Add. 14,706

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/572
Description based on Wright DXX (1:396-397)
Date: 13th century
East Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 119 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Funeral Services
This manuscript contains: The Funeral Service for all ranks. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 29 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 1/2 in. by 6 7/8, consisting of 119 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1—8 and 119. The quires, signed with letters, were originally 13 in number, but the first is now lost, and the last is imperfect. There are from 24 to 30 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, East Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, though rather inelegant, Nestorian hand of the xiiith cent.
