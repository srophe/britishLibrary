Add. 14,629, foll. 1–24

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/372
Description based on Wright DCCLXXVIII (2:754-756)
Date: 8th or 9th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 24 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Severus of Antioch. The concluding portion of an abridgment of the treatise of Severus of Antioch against Joannes Grammaticus, comprising book iii., chapp. xxxix.—xli.; John I., patriarch of Antioch. A Plerophoria, or Defence of the Faith, against the heresy of Julian of Halicarnassus , by John I., patriarch of Antioch; Of the fraudulent ordination of the Julianist bishops; How the above-mentioned Stephen made use of his episcopal dignity; Thomas, abbat of the convent of Mar Isaac. Letter of Thomas, abbat of the convent of Mar Isaac. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 11 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 7/8 in. by 6 1/2, consisting of 24 leaves, of which two (foll. 1 and 8) are much torn. The original signatures of the quires do not appear. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 33 to 42 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is neat and regular, of the viiith or ixth cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Addition 1 (Fol. 5a): A note on fol. 5 a states that the book belonged to the abbat Bar-Yeshua, bishop of the Arabs in al-Jazira (Mesopotamia), and that it was written by one 'Ebed-Yeshua', whose name is recorded in arithmetical figures, ܐܝܬܝܗ̇ ܕܝܢ ܦܢܩܝܬܐ ܗܕܐ̣. ܕܚܣܝܐ ܐܒܣ ܡܪܝ ܒܪܝܫܘܥ ܐܦܣܝܩܘܦܐ ܕܛܝ̈ܝܐ ܕܓܙܝܪܬܐ. ܐܝܟ ܕܠܝܘܬܪܢܐ ܕܝܠܗ̣ ܘܕܟܠ ܕܩ̇ܪܐ ܒܗ̇. ܣܪܛ ܕܝܢ ܚܛܝܐ ܘܡܣܟܢܐ̣. ܘܣܢܝܩ ܥܠ ܨ̈ܠܘܬܐ ܘܒ̈ܥܘܬܐ ܕܟܠܗܘܢ ܩܕܝ̈ܫܐ. ܝܬܝܪܐܝܬ ܕܩܕܝܫܬܐ ܘܡܫܒܚܬܐ ܘܒܬܘܠܬ ܒܟܠܙܒܢ ܛܘܒܢܝܬܐ ܘܝܠܕܬ ܐܠܗܐ ܡܪܝܡ̣. ܘܕܟܠܗܘܢ ܡܗܝ̈ܡܢܐ ܫܪܝܪ̈ܐ ܘܪ̈ܚ̇ܡܝ ܠܡܫܝܚܐ܀ ܫܒܥܝܢ ܬܪܝܢ ܐܪܒܥܐ ܥܣܪܐ ܬܠܬܡܐܐ ܫܬܐ ܫܒܥܝܢ. ܡܛܠ ܐܠܗܐ ܟܠ ܕܩ̇ܪܐ̣ ܢܨܠܐ ܥܠ ܚܣܝܐ ܡܪܝ ܐܒܣ܀ ܘܥܠܝ ܚܛܝܐ ܕܣ̇ܪܛܬ. ܘܡܛܠ ܐܠܗܐ ܠܐ ܬܥܕܠܘܢܢܝ ܟܠ ܕܩ̇ܪܐ. ܕܐܡ̇ܪ ܕܛܥܘ̈ܢܐ ܐܝܬ ܒܗ ܒܗܢܐ ܟܬܒܐ. ܕܐܢܐ ܐܝܟ ܕܐܝܬܘܗܝ ܗܘܐ ܒܨܚܚܐ ܕܝܠܗ̣ ܗܟܢ ܟܬܒܬܗ. ܟܕ ܠܐ ܒܨ̇ܪܬ ܐܘ ܐܘ̇ܣܦܬ ܡܢ ܨܚܚܗ. ܐܠܐ ܐܢ ܛܒܐܝܬ ܘܐܢ ܒܝܫܐܝܬ݂. ܐܝܟ ܕܐܫܟ̇ܚܬ ܒܨܚܚܐ̣ ܗܟܢ ܣܪܛ̇ܬ. ܏ܘܫ

Add. 14,629, foll. 25–31

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/373
Description based on Wright DCCCCLI (3:1111)
Date: ca. 9th century
East Syriac script ܀ Parchment Other, 7 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Lives
This manuscript contains: Part of a discourse on the persecutions and sufferings of the Christian martyrs; Part of the martyrdom of Cyprian and Justa. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 2 items.

Physical Description

Seven vellum leaves, about 9 3/4i n. by 6 1/4, all of which are much stained by water, and the last two much torn. The number of lines in each page varies from 33 to 37.

Hand 1 (sole, East Syriac script): They are written in a good, Nestorian hand of about the ixth cent., with many Syriac vowel-points.
