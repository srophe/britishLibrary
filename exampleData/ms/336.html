Add. 14,600

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/336
Description based on Wright DCXCIII (2:565-569)
Date: 8th century
Origin:
Parchment Codex, 148 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: Severus of Antioch. The sixth book of the select epistles of Severus of Antioch; . In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 50 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 11 in. by 7 3/8, consisting of 148 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1—3, 52, 53, 144, 146 and 147. The quires, signed with letters, were originally at least 22 in number, but ܐ, ܝܓ, ܝܕ and ܝܗ are lost, and, ܒ, ܙ, ܚ, ܛ and ܝ are very imperfect. Leaves are wanting both at the beginning and end, as well as after foll. 1, 53, 55, 57, 63, 84, 144, 145, 146 and 147. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 27 to 40 lines. This volume is written in a good, regular Estrangela of the viiith cent., and many Greek proper names and other words are expressed on the margins in Greek letters.
