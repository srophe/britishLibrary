Add. 17,182, foll. 1–99

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/835
Description based on Wright DXXIX (2:403-404)
Date: AD 474 / AG 785
Origin: Edessa
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 99 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Aphraates. The first part of the works of Aphraates. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 12 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 ¼ in. by 8 1/8, consisting of 99 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1, 2, 8, 40—42, and 99. The quires, 13 in number, were origi¬nally signed with both letters and arith¬metical figures (e. g. fol. 16 a, ܓand %) but in most cases either the one, or the other, or both have disappeared. A later hand has erroneously numbered them from ܐ to ܝܒ. Leaves are wanting after foll.l, 42, 43, 51, and 73. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 23 to 26 lines.. 7 is a later insertion, of about the ixth cent.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular, though by no means elegant Estrangela, and dated A. Gr. 785, A.D. 474.See additions.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Hand 4 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Hand 5 (minor, Unspecified Arabic script): See additions.
Hand 6 (minor, Unspecified Coptic script): See additions.
Hand 7 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Decoration (Ornamentation): Fol. 1b contains one or two ornamental designs in black ink

Add. 17,182, foll. 100–175

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/836
Description based on Wright DXXX (2:404-405)
Date: AD 512 / AG 823
Unspecified Latin script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 75 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Aphraates. The second part of the works of Aphraates; viz. homilies ii-xxii, and the treatise on "the Cluster.". In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 12 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 7/8 in. by 8 ¼ , consisting of 76 leaves (Add. 17,182, foll. 100—174), a few of which are slightly stained and torn, especially foll. 100—102 and 173—174. The quires were once probably 12 in number; of these the first two are lost, and the third is imperfect, a leaf being missing after fol. 105. The margins have been so closely cut that the original signatures are no longer extant. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 30 to 40 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): This manuscript is written in a fine, regular Estrangela, and dated A. Gr. 823, A. D. 512.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): One Abraham bar Nonnus has recorded his name.
Hand 3 (sole, Unspecified Latin script): Each side of fol.175 is in ancient Latin characters, not later than the vith cent.
