Add. 14,516

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/210
Description based on Wright CCCVIII (1:244-246)
Date: 9th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 208 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: A collection of Choral Services and Homilies for the principal festivals of the whole year. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 75 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 5/8 in. by 8 1/2, consisting of 208 leaves, many of which are much stained, defaced and torn, especially foll. 1, 6, 7, 9, 10, 16, 17, 88, 89, 91, 93, 98, 100, 112, 118, 127, 132, 185 and 198—208. The quires are signed with letters, but many of them are now lost. This volume is imperfect both at the beginning and the end, and there are lacunae of greater or less extent in various places. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 31 to 36 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): It is written in a neat, regular hand of the ixth cent.
