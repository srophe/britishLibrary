Add. 14,563

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/294
Description based on Wright DLXXXIX (2:471)
Date: 7th or 8th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 84 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: John Chrysostom. The Commentary of John Chrysostom on the first epistle of S. Paul to the Corinthians, homm. homilies xx.-xxxiii. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 in. by 6 3/4, consisting of 84 leaves, of which the first two are slightly stained and torn. The quires, eleven in number, are signed with both letters and arithmetical figures (e.g. fol.. 5, ܕ; fol. 15, ܗ; fol. 65, ܝـ; fol. 75, ܝܐ). Leaves are wanting at the beginning and end, as well as after foll. 1, 2, and 3. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 31 to 41 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, regular hand of the viith or viiith cent.

Manuscript Contents

Note: Item numbering updated in digital edition.
Item 1 (Fol. 1a): John Chrysostom. The Commentary of John Chrysostom on the first epistle of S. Paul to the Corinthians, homm. homilies xx.-xxxiii. [defective]
Notes:
  • Of homm. [homilies] xx. and xxi. only a very small portion remains, fol.1; See Opera, t. x., pp. 208, l. 33 -210, l. 23. Of hom. [homily] xxii. but a single leaf is left, fol.2, containing t. x., pp. 228, l. 29—230, l. 12. Hom. [Homily] xxiii. commences on fol.3a, with t. x., p. 236, l. 26, but there is a lacuna after fol.3, extending from p. 238, l. 12, to p. 239, l. 39. Hom. [Homily] xxxiii. ends with t. x., p. 350, line 47.
  • Cf. Opera, t. x., p. 197; Migne, Patrol. Gr., t. lxi., col. 159
