Add. 17,151

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/780
Description based on Wright DCXIX (2:490-491)
Date: 7th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 109 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Cyril of Alexandria. The second part of the first volume of the treatise of Cyril of Alexandria, on Worship in Spirit and in Truth, viz. books v.-viii. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 3/8 in. by 6 3/4, consisting of 109 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, were 14 in number, but ܒ, ܓ, and ܛ, are entirely lost, leaving lacunae after foll. 10 and 60. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 27 to 32 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular Estrangělā of the viith cent. See additions.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions
Hand 4 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions
Hand 5 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions

Book vi. commences, fol.11a, with the words (Opera, ed. Aubert, t. i., p. 172 B)ἀναδησόμεθα ψῆφον, ῆ οὐ δοκῶ σοι φρονεῖν ὀρθὰ, τὸ ἔν γε τούτοις φιλοκρινεῖν ἡρημένοις; In book vii., alter fol.60, there is a lacuna, extending from Opera, ed. Aubert, t. i., p. 223 E, ἰσχυρότεραι δὲ προβαίνουσαι τῆς σύλπιγγος αἱ φωναὶ, to Opera, ed. Aubert, t. i., p. 234 .D, εἰρῆσθαι δὲ καὶ πρὸς ἡμῶν αὐτῶν ἁγίῳ παντὶ, τοὺς πόνους τῶν καρπῶν σου φάγεσαι.
