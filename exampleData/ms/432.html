Add. 14,664, foll. 1–17, 20–21

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/433
Description based on Wright CCLIV (1:203)
Date: 10 or 11th century
Christian Palestinian Aramaic script ܀ Parchment Other, 19 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Lectionaries
This manuscript contains: Fragments of a Lectionary from the Gospels. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Nineteen vellum leaves, about 9 5/8 in. by 8, all more or less stained and mutilated (Add. 14,664, foll. 1—17, 20, 21). They are written in double columns, in the Palestinian character and dialect, and belong apparently to the xth or xith cent.

Hand 1 (sole, Christian Palestinian Aramaic script): The original text is written in the Palestinian character and dialect, and belong apparently to the 10 or 11th century.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1): Fragments of a Lectionary from the Gospels [defective]
Note: Similar to that contained in the Vatican manuscript, edited by Count Miniscalchi Erizzo(Evangeliarium Hierosolymitanum ex Cod. Vat.. Palaestino etc., Verona, 1801—64). See below,Part 5. An edition of these fragments is in the course of preparation of Dr. Land.

Add. 14,664, foll. 3–7, 9–17, upper text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1187
Description based on Wright CCLIV (1:203)
Date: 13th century
Estrangela script ܀ Parchment Other, 14 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Fragments of the Gospel of S. John, according to the Harklensian version.; Timotheus of Jerusalem. Portions of the discourse ofTimotheus ofJerusalem onS. Luke, ch. 2:25—35. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 2 items.

Physical Description

Vellum leaves, about 9 5/8 in. by 8. These leaves are palimpsest (see Add. 14,664, foll, 1-17, 20, 21 ), with the exception of foll. 1, 2, 8, 20, and 21; Foll. 6, 10, and 15, are re-written on one side only.

Hand 1 (sole, Estrangela script): The more modern text is written in a large, coarse Estrangela of about the 13th century.

Add. 14,664, foll. 18–19, 30–32, lower text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/434
Description based on Wright DVI (1:389)
Date: 10th century
Christian Palestinian Aramaic script ܀ Parchment Other, 6 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Hymns in praise of martyrs. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Six vellum leaves, about 7 in. by 5, all of which but one are much torn (Add. 14,664, foll. 18, 19, 30—33). With the exception of fol. 33, they are palimpsest; but foll. 19 b and 31 a, as well as part of fol. 30 b, have not been re-written. For the more modern texts, see Add. 14,664 foll. 18-19, 30-32, upper text .

Hand 1 (sole, Christian Palestinian Aramaic script): The older text is written in the Palestinian character, in a large, regular hand, perhaps of the 10th century.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 18): Hymns in praise of martyrs

Add. 14,664, foll. 22–29

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/435
Description based on Wright LXII (1:39)
Date: 10th or 11th century
Christian Palestinian Aramaic script ܀ Parchment Other, 8 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Old Testament
This manuscript contains: Fragments of a manuscript of the Psalms, containing a translation from the Septuagint, in the Palestinian dialect. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Eight vellum leaves, about 6 7/8 in. by 5 1/4, all more or less stained and torn. They are neatly written in the Palestinian character, and seem to be of the xth or xith cent. There are from 15 to 18 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Christian Palestinian Aramaic script): They are neatly written in the Palestinian character, and seem to be of the 10th or 11th century.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 22): Fragments of a manuscript of the Psalms, containing a translation from the Septuagint, in the Palestinian dialect. [defective]
