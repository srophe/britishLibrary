Add. 17,131

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/760
Description based on Wright DXVI (1:395)
Date: 12th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 59 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Funeral Services
This manuscript contains: Funeral Services. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 7 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 11 1/4 in. by 7 1/4, consisting of 59 leaves, the first and last of which are blank. Foll. 1—3 have been almost completely torn out, and fol. 58 is slightly injured. The quires, signed with letters, are 6 in number. A single leaf is wanting after fol. 58. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 20 to 24 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written, like its companion volume, Add. 14,502, in a good, regular hand of the xiith cent., with occasional Syriac and Greek vowels.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Most of the Syriac and Greek vowels have been added at a later period.
