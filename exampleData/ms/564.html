Add. 14,698

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/564
Description based on Wright CCCXLIII (1:288-289)
Date: 12th or 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 98 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: A Tropologion or collection of Greek Canons and Syrian Anthems for the first half of the year. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 32 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 13 in. by 9, consisting of 98 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 87—98. The quires, signed with letters, seem to have been originally 20 in number, but the last eleven are very imperfect, leaves being wanting after foll. 87, 90, 91, 92, 93, 94, and 95. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 28 to 42 lines. Green and yellow paints have occasionally been used in writing the rubrics of this volume; e.g. fol. 42 b.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good regular hand of the xiith or xiiith cent
