Add. 14,651, upper text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1453
Description based on Wright DCCCCXLVIII (3:1101-1103)
Date: AD 850 / AG 1161
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 217 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Lives
This manuscript contains: Lives of Saints and Martyrs; Discourses by several writers; The doxology; Index of the contents of the volume; Further discourses by several writers. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 23 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, 8 1/4 in. by 5 1/8, consisting of 217 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, are 21 in number. A single leaf is wanting after fol. 171. There are from 19 to 26 lines in each page. This volume is palimpsest throughout (see below).

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The upper text is written in a good, regular hand, and dated A. Gr. 1161, A.D. 850.

Date: 6th or 7th century
Estrangela script ܀ Parchment Codex, 217 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: The Gospels, according to the Peshitta version. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 2 items.

Physical Description

This manuscript is palimpsest throughout. The lower texts' quires are signed with letters; e. g. foll. 31 a, ܓ, 181 a, ܒ.

Hand 1 (sole, Estrangela script): The ancient text is written in a fine, large Estrangela of the vith or viith cent.
Decoration (Rubrication):Most of the rubrics, being painted with red paint, have either nearly or altogether disappeared.
Decoration (Illustration): Fol. 112 b exhibits a Cross within an oval nimbus, which was probably once decorated with various colours.
Decoration (Illustration): There is a similar Cross, but ofsmaller size, on fol. 216 a.
