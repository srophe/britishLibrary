Add. 14,694, foll. 1–43

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/559
Description based on Wright CCLXVI (1:209)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 43 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Missals
This manuscript contains: A small collection of Anaphoras. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 5 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 8 1/8 in. by 5 1/2, consisting of 43 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, are five in number, the last being imperfect. There are from 13 to 19 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a rather inelegant hand of the xiiith cent.

Contents

Add. 14,694, foll. 44–105

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/560
Description based on Wright CCLXVII (1:209-210)
Date: 13th century
(multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 63 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Missals
This manuscript contains: A collection of Anaphoras. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 7 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 8 1/8 in. by 5 1/2, consisting of 63 leaves (Add. 14,694, foll. 44—105), some of which are slightly torn, especially foll. 88—95. The quires, signed with letters, are six in number. There are from 12 to 22 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written, apparently, by two hands of the xiiith cent. (foll. 44—95 a and 95 b—105).

Contents

This manuscript contains 7 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.
