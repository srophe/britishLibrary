Add. 17,154

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/783
Description based on Wright DCXCI (2:557-558)
Date: 7th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 52 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Sergius Grammaticus, Severus of Antioch. The correspondence of Sergius Grammaticus andSeverus of Antioch, regarding the doctrine of the Two Natures in Christ. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 8 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 7/8 in. by 6 5/8, consisting of 52 leaves, some of which are slightly stained, especially at the beginning. The quires, signed with letters, are 6 in number. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 31 to 42 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): This volume is written in a good, regular Estrangělā of the viith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions

Manuscript Contents

Note: Item numbering updated in digital edition.
Item 1 (Fol. 1): Sergius Grammaticus, Severus of Antioch. The correspondence of Sergius Grammaticus and Severus of Antioch, regarding the doctrine of the Two Natures in Christ
Note: The writings of Cyril of Alexandria are quoted on almost every page of these letters. Psalm xcii., fol. 42 b; Gregory Nazianzen; Julius of Rome, foll.7 b, 27 b; Leo of Rome, fol. 49 b; Nestorius, foll. 24 b, 49 b; Theodoret, fol. 49 b; and the Acts of the Council of Chalcedon, fol. 50 a.

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 1a): According to a note on fol. 1a, the book was presented to the convent of S. Mary Deipara by one Michael bar Kattā'ē. ܐܝܬܘܗܝ ܟܬܒܐ ܗܢܐ ܕܡܝܟܐܝܠ ܒܪ ܩ̈ܛܥܐ: ܕܫܟܢܗ ܠܕܝܪܐ ܕܣܘܪ̈ܝܝܐ ܕܒܡܕܒܪܐ ܕܐܣ̈ܩܝܛܐ ܕܡܨܪ̈ܝܢ܀ ܕܐܠܗܐ ܢܢܝܚ ܢܦܫܗ ܒܐܘ̈ܢܐ ܕܟ̈ܐܢܐ ܘܕܙܕܝ̈ܩܐ. ܘܢܟܬܘܒ ܫܡܗ ܒܣܦܪܐ ܕܚ̈ܝܐ. ܏ܘܫ.
