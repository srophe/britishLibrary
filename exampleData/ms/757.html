Add. 17,128

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/757
Description based on Wright CCXC (1:226-227)
Date: 10th or 11th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 192 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Sacerdotals
This manuscript contains: A Sacerdotal. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 68 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 1/2 in. by 7 3/8, consisting of 192 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1, 66, 156, and 166. The quires, signed with letters, are, according to the present signatures, 22 in number. Leaves are wanting at the beginning, and after foll. 1, 2, 65, 73, 84, 124, 126, 140, 156, 165, 172, 175, 176, 178, 181, and 182. There are from 26 to 35 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is good and regular, of the xth or xith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Foll. 1—10, 66—70, and 183—192, are somewhat later than the rest.

Additions

N.B., items below may have been listed out of folio order in Wright's catalogue.
Addition 1 (Fol. 176b): That this manuscript became defective at an early period may be seen from the marginal note on fol. 176 b: ܐܚܘܢ ܥܒܪ ܐܪ̈ܒܥܐ ܛܪ̈ܦܐ ܘܒܪܝܫܗ ܕܗ̇ܘ ܛܪܦܐ ܕܩܛܝܥ ܡܫܟܚ ܐܢܬ ܚܘܬܡܗ ܕܗܢ ܣܕܪܐ ܗܢܐ
Addition 2 (Fol. 90a): On the margin of fol. 90 a, we find the name of Zacchaeus ܙܟܝ ܚܛܝܐ
Addition 3 (Fol. 174b): on the margin of fol. 174 b, we find the name of Bar-saumā ܒܪܨܘܡܐ ܚܛܝܐ
Addition 4 (Fol. 158a): On the margin of fol. 158 a, there is written, in Coptic letters, the name of Simeon, ⲤⲨⲘⲈⲰⲚ; followed by that of Abba Bīshōi, ⲀⲂⲂⲀ ⲠⲒϢⲰⲒ, and then the words ⲪϮ ⲚⲀⲒⲚⲀϤ, "God be merciful to him!" ⲤⲨⲘⲈⲰⲚ, ⲀⲂⲂⲀ ⲠⲒϢⲰⲒ, ⲪϮ ⲚⲀⲒⲚⲀϤ
