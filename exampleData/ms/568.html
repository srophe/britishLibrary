Add. 14,702

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/568
Description based on Wright CCCLXXXV (1:309-310)
Date: 15th century AD 1489 / AG 1800
Origin: S. Mary Deipara
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 53 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: A Choir-book, Containing Services for the Commemoration of Saints, etc., similar to those in the preceding numbers. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 6 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 1/8 in. by 6 3/4, consisting of 53 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, are six in number. There are from 12 to 21 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is neatly written by two hands (the former comprising foll. 2—50) of the xvth cent.,
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The latter portion, foll. 51— 53, being dated A. Gr. 1800, A.D. 1489.

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 1a): On fol. 1a there are two neatly written passages from the Gospels, in Arabic, viz. S. Mark, ch. xii. 43, 44, and S. Matthew, ch. viii. 11—15. These seem to be of the same date as the rest of the volume. (%)
Addition 2 (Fol. 53a): The colophon,fol. 53 a, gives the name of the second scribe, Moses of Mount Lebanon, who wrote in the year 1800 (A.D. 1489), in the convent of S.
