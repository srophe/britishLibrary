Add. 14,691, foll. 1–109

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/550
Description based on Wright CCLXIV (1:208-209)
Date: AD 1230 / AG 1541
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 109 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Missals
This manuscript contains: A lesson (1 Corinth., xi. 23 — 32); Several prayers (ܨ̈ܠܘܬܐ ܕܒܘܪܟܬܐ); Instructions to the priest for celebrating the holy Eucharist; A collection of Anaphoras. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 13 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 1/4 in. by 6 7/8, consisting of 109 leaves, many of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1—8 and 107—109. The quires, signed with letters, are 11 in number. There are from 14 to 21 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is good and regular.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Foll. 1 and 2 a were written by a different hand from the rest of the volume.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): A later hand has added the anaphora of Eustathius.
Hand 4 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Additions

N.B., items below may have been listed out of folio order in Wright's catalogue.

Addition 2 (Fol. 73b): In another note, at the foot of fol. 73 b, he mentions his brother Rabban Michael. ܐܠܗܐ ܢܚܣܐ ܠܟܬܘܒܟ ܥܡ ܐܒܗ̈ܘܗܝ ܪ̈ܘܚܢܝܐ ܘܦܓܪ̈ܢܝܐ. ܘ܏ܫ. ܘܠܐܚܘܢ ܪܒܢ ܡܝܟܐܝܠ ܡܪܝܐ ܡܚܣܐ ܠܗ ܕܐܦ ܗܘ ܐܫܬܘܬܦ ܥܡܝ.
Addition 3 (Fol. 108a): The volume passed by purchase from the hands of ‘Adlēv into those of Rabban Iyūb. Fol. 108 a. ܙܒܢ ܟܘܪܣܐ ܗܢܐ ܕܩܘܪ̈ܒܐ ܪܒܢ ܐܝܘܒ ܒܡܕܡ ܕܙܢܬ ܠܗ ܛܝܒܘܬܐ ܡܢ ܪܒܢ ܥܕܠܘ ܐܟܡܐ ܕܢܩܪܒ ܒܗ ܘܢܠܦ ܡܢܗ ܐܠܗܐ ܢܚܣܐ ܠܟܬܘܒܐ ܘܠܙܒܘܢܐ ܘܠܡܢ ܕܡܩܪܒ ܒܗ ܐܝܢ ܘܐܡܝܢ ܘܠܐܠܗܐ ܫܘܒܚܐ.
Addition 4 (Fol. 108a): Rabban Iyūb in his turn sold it to Rabban Joseph. Fol. 108 a. ܙܒܢ ܠܟܘܪܣܐ ܗܢܐ ܕܩܘܪ̈ܒܐ ܪܒܢ ܝܘܣܦ ܡܢ ܪܒܢ ܐܝܘܒ ܒܡܕܡ ܕܙܢܬ݀ ܠܗ ܛܝܒܘܬܐ ܐܟܡܐ ܕܢܩܪܒ ܒܗ ܘܢܐܬܪ ܡܢܗ ܘܒܥܐ ܡܢ ܟܠ ܐܚܐ ܦܪܘܫܐ ܕܦܓܥ ܒܗ ܐܡ̇ܪ ܒܚܘܒܐ ܐܠܗܐ ܢܚܣܐ ܠܙܒܘܢܐ ܘܠܡܙܒܢܢܐ ܏ܘܫ.

Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Other, 5 leaves.
