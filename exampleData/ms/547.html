Add. 14,689

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/547
Description based on Wright CCXXVII (1:167-169)
Date: AD 1221 / AG 1532
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 187 leaves.
Features: Decoration
Wright's Subject Classification: Lectionaries
This manuscript contains: The Consecration of the Church; The Annunciation of Zacharias; The Annunciation of the blessed Virgin; The Visit of Mary to Elisabeth; The Nativity of S. John the Baptist; .... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 81 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 1/8 in. by 6 7/8, consisting of 187 leaves, many of which are much soiled and torn, especially foll. 1—5 and 141—187. The quires, 19 in number, are signed with letters from ܐ to ܩ. Leaves are wanting at the beginning and after fol. 4. There are from 12 to 24 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Foll 1-76 was written by one scribe.
Hand 2 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): Foll 77-187 was written by another scribe.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Decoration (Rubrication):
Decoration (Ornamentation):

N.B., items below may have been listed out of folio order in Wright's catalogue.
