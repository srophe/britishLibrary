Add. 12,158

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/33
Description based on Wright DCXC (2:555-557)
Date: 6th century, probably AD 588 / AG 899
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 128 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: Severus of Antioch. Against the Additions, or Appendices, of Julian, in 43 chapters; An index of Against the last Apology of Julian; Severus of Antioch. Against the last Apology of Julian. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 4 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 12 1/8 in. by 9 3/8, consisting of 128 leaves, some of which are much mutilated, especially foll. 1—3 and 128. The quires, signed with letters, were originally 16 in number, but several leaves of ܐ and ܒ, and the whole of ܓ and ܕ are now lost. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 28 to 37 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): This volume is written in a fine, clear Estrangělā of the vith cent., the exact date being, in all probability, A. Gr. 899, A.D. 588.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 127b): At the foot of fol. 127 b, a later hand has written over an erasure these words: "This book was finished in the convent of . . . . , in the year 899 (A.D. 588), in the days of the abbat Mār Thomas. May God" etc. ܐܫܬܠܡ ܕܝܢ ܟܬܒܐ ܗܢܐ ܒܕܝܪܐ ܕܡܟܝ (or ܕܡܟܖ) ܒܫܢܬ ܬܡܢܡܐܐ ܘܬܫܥܝܢ ܘܬܫܥ. ܒܝܘ̈ܡܝ ܪܝܫ ܕܝܪܐ ܡܪܝ ܬܘܡܐ. ܕܐܠܗܐ ܢܚܣܐ ܠܗ ܘܠܟܠ ܕܝܨܦ ܘܥܡܠ ܘܢܘܪܬ ܠܟܠܗܘܢ ܚ̈ܝܐ ܘܡܠܟܘܬܐ ܕܫܡܝܐ ܐܝܢ ܘܐܡܝܢ ܘܐܡܝܢ
Addition 2: The greater part of fol. 128 having been cut away, the note which it contained is lost, with the exception of a few unimportant lines at the top of the second column.
Addition 3 (Fol. 1a): On fol. 1 a there is a long note, in a hand of the xth cent., from which we learn that the volume was presented to the convent of S.
