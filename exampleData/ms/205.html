Add. 14,511

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/205
Description based on Wright CCCXI (1:249-250)
Date: 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 145 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Ephraim, Jacob of Batnae. A collection of Choral Services, consisting of ܡ̈ܥܢܝܬܐ, ܡܕܪ̈ܫܐ, ܣܘ̈ܓܝܬܐ, ܒܥ̈ܘܬܐ, etc., for the festivals of the whole year. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 27 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 8 3/4 in. by 5 7/8, consisting of 145 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, are now 15 in number, but several are wanting at the end of the volume, and two leaves are missing after fol. 3. There are from 19 to 27 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): From fol. 1 to 119 this manuscript is written in a good, regular hand of the xth cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): And the remainder, which is also very neatly written, is of about the same age.
