Add. 12,147

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/22
Description based on Wright CCCXX (1:261-264)
Date: AD 1006 / AG 1317
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Mixed Material Codex, 359 leaves.
Features: Decoration
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Ephraim, Jacob. The second part of a Choral Service-book for the circle of the whole year. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 79 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, 16 5/8 in. by 12 5/8, consisting of 359 leaves, many of which are stained and torn. Originally the number of quires must have been about forty, fol. 346 being signed ܠܚ; but not a few are now defective, especially from fol. 299 onwards. In the earlier part of the volume there are lacunae after foll. 10, 27 and 296.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The hand is the same as that of Add. 12,146.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): As in Add. 12,146, some of the madrashe are ascribed on the margins, by later hands, to Ephraim (e.g. foll. 170 a and 171 b) and Jacob (e.g. fol. 135 b), whilst the bā'ăvāthā are attributed in the text to either the one or the other, according to the metre.
Hand 4 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Foll. 4, 5, 32, 33, 55, 56, 77, 78, 102, 103, 127 and 128, are supplementary paper leaves, inserted apparently during the xiiith cent.

Addition 5 (Fol. 68b): To a marginal addition on fol. 68 b are appended the words: "written by Rabban Athanasius (or #Theōnas?), the disciple of our blessed Father; may the Lord grant him forgiveness." ܟܬ݂ܒ ܪܒܢ ܐܬܐܘܢܘܣ ܬܠܡܝܕܐ ܕܐܒܘܢ ܛ݁ܘܒܬܢܐ. ܡܪܝܐ ܢܚܲܣܐ ܠܗ
