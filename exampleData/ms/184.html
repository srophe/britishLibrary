Add. 14,494

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/184
Description based on Wright CCLXXXIV (1: 217-218)
Date: 9th or 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 108 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Sacerdotals
This manuscript contains: A Sacerdotal, or book containing everything necesary for the Offices of a Priest. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 18 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 6 3/4 in. by 4 7/8, consisting of 108 leaves, some of which are stained and torn, especially foll. 1—4 and 95—98. The quires, signed with letters, were originally at least 15 in number (fol. 108 b, ܝܕ); but several of them are either imperfect or altogether wanting, there being lacunae after foll. 1, 3, 4, 8, 9, 15, 26, 34, 94 and 98. Each page has from 20 to 26 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is small and neat, of the ixth or xth cent., except fol. 1, which is somewhat later.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Fol. 1 was written somewhat later than the rest of the manuscript.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 1a): On fol. 1 a there are two notes. Of the first, which has been designedly erased, nothing is distinctly legible but a few words in the last line. ܕܕܝܪܐ ܕܣܘܪ̈ܝܝܐ ܕܐܣ̈ܩܝܛܐ
