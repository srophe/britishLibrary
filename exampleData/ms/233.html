Add. 14,524, foll. 12–21

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/238
Description based on Wright CCCCXXXII (1:349)
Date: 11th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Other, 10 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Part of the Hymns of Severus; Part of the Hymns (ܦܢܩܝܬܐ ܕܡ̈ܥܢܝܬܐ) of Severus. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 2 items.

Physical Description

Ten vellum leaves, about 6 in. by 3 7/8, forming the first quire of a neatly written manuscript of the xith cent., with ornamental devices (Add. 14,524, foll. 12—21). There are from 20 to 24 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): Neatly written, of the xith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 2 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 12b): Part of the Hymns of Severus
Note: Foll. 12 b and 13 a contain the hymns of Severus , but according to a different arrangement, which the scribe presently abandoned, commencing afresh on fol. 13 b.
Item 2 (Fol. 13b): Part of the Hymns (ܦܢܩܝܬܐ ܕܡ̈ܥܢܝܬܐ) of Severus
 
Title: ܒܚܝ̣ܠ ܥܘܕܪܢܐ ܕܟܝ̇ܢܐ ܗ̇ܘ ܬܠܝܬ݂ܝ ܩ̈ܢܘܡܐ ܕܐܒܐ ܘܕܒܪܐ ܘܕܪܘܚܐ ܩܕܝܫܐ. ܡܫ̇ܪܝܢܢ ܕܢܟܬ݂ܘܒ ܦܢܩܝܬܐ ܕܡ̈ܥܢܝܬܐ ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܡܪܝ ܣܐܘܝܪܐ ܦܐܛܪܝܪܟܐ ܕܐܢܛܝܘܟܝܐ. ܐܝܟ ܬ݁ܘܪܨܐ ܚ̣ܕܬܐ ܟܠ ܚܕܐ ܒܬܪ ܒܪܬ ܫܘܼܒܚܗ
Note: These hymns are for the Nativity, ܕܒܝܬ ܝܠ̣ܕܐ, 14 in number, and the first three for the Epiphany, ܕܒܝܬ ܕܢܚ̣ܐ. Each hymn is accompanied by letters on the margin, indicating its number and the tone according to which it is chanted.
