Add. 14,517

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/211
Description based on Wright DII (1:385-388)
Date: 10th or 11th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 117 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Prayers
This manuscript contains: A large collection of Prayers for the various festivals, the daily services, etc. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 127 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 1/2 in. by 7 1/4, consisting of 117 leaves, a few of which are much stained and soiled. The quires, signed with letters, were originally 14 in number (ܟܕ to ܠܙ), but two are lost ܟܚ and ܠܗ, and they have been re-numbered from ܟܓ to ܠܕ . There are from 27 to 40 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume, which is written by the same hands as Add. 17,128 (see above, p. 226, no. ccxc), belongs to the x or xi cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Estrangela script): The prayer to be said before the hymn "Gloria in excelsis" and the addition beneath it are written in large Estrangela characters.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
