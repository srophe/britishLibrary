Physical Description

Vellum, about 12 3/8 in. by9 ¼ consisting of 135 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, are seventeen in number. A few loaves at the beginning and end aroundmuch stained and torn, and one leaf is missing after fol. 127. Each page is divided into three columns, of from 25 to 34 lines. This manuscript is written in a fine,bold Estrangela, and dated A. Gr. 915, A.D. 604

Additions

N.B., items below may have been listed out of folio order in Wright's catalogue.
Addition 1 (Fol. 135a): A note on fol. 135a, in a more cursive character, states that this manuscript was written for the recluse Sha'dūn, residing near the village of ܡܬܢ, in the province of Bostra, A. Gr. 915=A.D. 604, who gave for it the sum of 4 dīnārs, minus 3 carats. ܐܬܚܦܛ ܘܥܒܕ ܟܬܒܐ ܗܢܐ ܪܚܡ ܐܠܗܐ ܡܪܝ ܫܥܕܘܢ ܚܒܝܫܐ ܕܒܟܘܪܐ ܕܒܘܨܪܐ ܕܥܡܪ ܒܬܚܘܡܐ ܕܡܬܢ ܩܪܝܬܐ ܡܢ ܕܐܠܗܐ ܘܡܢ ܕܝܠܗ. ܐܬܟܬܒ ܕܝܢ ܒܫܢܬ ܬܫܥܡܐܐ ܘܚܡܫܥܣܪܐ ܕܐܠܟܣܢܕܪܘܣ ܒܐܝܪܚ ܬܡܘܙ ܒܚܡܫܬܥܪ ܒܗ ܒܝܪܚܐ. ܐܙܕܒܢ ܕܝܢ ܛ̈ܝܡܐ ܕܝܠܗ ܕܝܢܪ̈ܐ ܐܪ̈ܒܥܐ ܒܨܝܪ ܩܪ̈ܛܐ ܬܠܬܐ. ܕܐܠܗܐ ܗܘ ܕܡܛܠ ܫܡܗ ܐܬܚܦܛ ܘܙܒܢ ܏ܘܫ.
