Add. 14,655

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/423
Description based on Wright DCCCCLVII (3:1119-1120)
Date: 11th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 99 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Lives
This manuscript contains: The History of Maximus and Domitius; The history of Archelides; The history of the Man of God from the city of Rome, in two parts; Ephraim. A metrical discourse of Ephraim; The Creed of the Orthodox; .... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 8 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 5 5/8 in. by 4 5/8, consisting of 99 leaves, some of which are much stained and slightly torn, especially foll. 34, 44, 54, 72, 92—94, and 99. The quires, signed with letters, were originally 16 in number, but several of them are now either wanting or very imperfect. Leaves are missing at the beginning, as well as after foll. 1, 2, 14, 32, 33, 34, 41, 42, 65, 70, 80, and 97. There are from 12 to 16 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, regular hand of about the xith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
