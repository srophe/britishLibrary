Add. 14,541, foll. 39–49

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/270
Description based on Wright DCXCIX (2:585-586)
Date: 8th or 9th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Other, 11 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: Theodosius, patriarch of Alexandria. The writings ofTheodosius, patriarch ofAlexandria; Severus of Antioch. A copy of a note, written bySeverus ofAntioch in a volume belonging to the library of the convent of the Dalmatians (?), atthe EnatonnearAlexandria. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 11 items.

Physical Description

Eleven vellum leaves, about 10 5/8 in. by 7 1/8, of which the first two and the last are much stained and torn (Add. 14,541, foll. 39—49). Each page is divided into two columns, of from 37 to 41 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): Written in a good, regular Estrangela of the viiith or ixth cent, See addition 1.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Greek script): On the margins of the leaves there are many words written in Greek letters, of which those given above may suffice as specimens.
