Add. 14,512, upper text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1402
Description based on Wright CCCXII (1:250-251)
Date: 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 144 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Choral Services for the principal festivals of the whole year, consisting ofܩ̈ܠܐ, ܡܕܪ̈ܫܐ, ܒ̈ܥܘܬܐ, etc., ascribed toEphraimandJacob. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 17 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 8 3/4 in. by 5 5/8, consisting of 144 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 43, 134, and 141— 144. The quires, signed with letters, are at present only 15 in number. The first six quires, the eighth, and several at the end of the volume, are altogether lost; and leaves are missing after foll. 1, 2, 3, 4, 14 and 22. There are from 19 to 24 lines in each page. This volume is palimpsest throughout, comprising parts of three manuscripts (see Add. 14,512 foll. 67-124, lower text , Add. 14,512, foll. 36-66 and 125-144, lower text , and Add. 14,512, foll. 1-35, lower text ).

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume, which is palimpsest throughout, is written in a good, regular hand of the Xth century.

Add. 14,512, foll. 67–124, lower text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1403
Description based on Wright CCCXII (1:250-251)
Date: 6th or 7th century
Origin: This manuscript is written in a fine Estrangela of the vith or viith cent.
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Other, 58 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: The book of Isaiah, according to the Peshitta version. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Vellum leaves, about 8 3/4 in. by 5 5/8, written in two columns. These leaves are palimpsest (see Add. 14,512, upper text ).

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): . A manuscript of the book of Isaiah, according to the Peshitta version, written in two columns, in a fine Estrangela of the vith or viith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 67a): The book of Isaiah, according to the Peshitta version [defective]
 
Title: ܐܫܥܝܐ
Note: The running title ܐܫܥܝܐ is still clearly visible on foll. 74 a, 83 a, 94 a, 96 a, 102 a, 103 a, at the foot; and on foll.75 a, 84 b, 100 b, 108 b, 113 b, 122 b, and 124 a, at the top. The text is most distinctly legible on foll. 69 and 72, the latter of which contains ch. xvi.1— xvii.2, and the former, ch. xvii.2—xviii. 3.
