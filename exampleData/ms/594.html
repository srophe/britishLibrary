Add. 14,717, foll. 1–58

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/595
Description based on Wright DXVIII (1:395-396)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 58 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Funeral Services
This manuscript contains: Funeral Services. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 9 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 8 ¾ in. by 5 ¾, consisting of 58 leaves, the first and last of which are much torn. The quires, signed with letters, must have been originally at least 9 in number, but the first two and the last are now missing. There is a lacuna between foll. 57 and 58. Each page has from 14 to 16 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a coarse, inelegant hand of the xiii cent.

Add. 14,717, foll. 59–102

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/596
Description based on Wright CCCLXXXVII (1:311)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 44 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Services for the Commemoration of Bar-saumā. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 6 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 9 in. by 6, consisting of 44 leaves (Add. 14,717, foll. 59—102), some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 59 and 96—102. The quires, signed with letters, are four in number, the first being imperfect at the beginning. There are from 17 to 21 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular hand of the xiiith cent.

Add. 14,717, foll. 103–130

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/597
Description based on Wright CCCLXXIX (1:307)
Date: 13th or 14th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 28 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: The first part of a Choir-book containing hymns (ܩ̈ܠܐ,ܡܕܪ̈ܫܐ) and prayers (ܒܥ̈ܘܬܐ) for the following occasions. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 6 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 9 1/8 in. by 6, consisting of 3 quires or 28 leaves (Add. 14,717, foll. 103 —130), several of which are slightly stained and torn. There are from 18 to 27 lines in each page. The first quire is in a different handwriting from the other two.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is of the xiiith or xivth cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The first quire is in a different handwriting from the other two.

Contents

This manuscript contains 6 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 103a): The first part of a Choir-book containing hymns (ܩ̈ܠܐ,ܡܕܪ̈ܫܐ) and prayers (ܒܥ̈ܘܬܐ) for the following occasions
 
Title: ܦܠܓܘܬܐ ܩܕܡܝܬܐ

Title: ܕܫܘܢܝܗ̇ ܕܝܠܕܬ ܐܠܗܐ

Title: ܕܥܐܕܐ ܕܡ̈ܛܠܠܐ
