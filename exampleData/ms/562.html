Add. 14,696

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/562
Description based on Wright CCCXLIV (1:289-290)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 315 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: A large collection of Canons and Anthems for the festivals of the whole year. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 53 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 13 1/2 in. by 10 1/8, consisting of 315 leaves, many of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1—5, 303, 304, and 308—315. The quires, signed with letters, were originally at least 34 in number, but ܐ is now entirely lost, and leaves are wanting after foll. 1, 2, 312, and 315. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 25 to 31 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, regular hand of the xiiith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Fol.302 is a somewhat later addition.
