Add. 14,667, foll. 1–12

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/463
Description based on Wright CLXIII (1:111)
Date: 10th century
West Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 12 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Punctuation
This manuscript contains: Part of a small book of the Vowel-points of the Scriptures. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 10 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 4 7/8 in. by 5, consisting of 12 leaves, most of which are more or less soiled and torn. The quires are signed with letters (ܒ, fol. 7b). There are from 21 to 24 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, West Syriac script): The writing is neat and regular, of about the xth cent., with numerous Greek vowels and other points ܰ, ܶ, ܺ, ܳ, ܽ, as ܠܰܡܕ̣ܩܘ, ܡ̇ܠܟ݂, ܕ̇ܬ݂ܘܽܩܦ݂ܳܐ).
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
