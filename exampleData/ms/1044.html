Add. 17,252

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1044
Description based on Wright CCCLVIII (1:296-297)
Date: 13th or 14th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 77 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Cathismata, ܩ̇ܐܬܝ̣̈ܣܡܐ or ܩܬܝ̈ܣܡܐ (καθίσματα), and other hymns, for various festivals. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 28 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 7 1/8 in. by 5 1/8, consisting of 77 leaves, a few of which are more or less torn, especially foll. 7, 8, and 68—77. The quires, signed with letters, were at least 11 in number; but the first four are very imperfect, there being large lacunae after foll. 6 and 7. There are from 15 to 28 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a current hand of the xiiith or xivth cent.
