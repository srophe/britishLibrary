Add. 14,519

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/213
Description based on Wright CCCXXVII (1:272-274)
Date: 11th or 12th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 201 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: A Choral Service-book for the circle of the whole year, comprising anthems (ܥܢܝ̈ܢܐ), chiefly for vespers (ܕܪܡܫܐ), nocturnes (ܕܠܠܝܐ), and morning prayer (ܕܨܦܪܐ). In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 69 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 3/8 in. by 7 3/4, consisting of 201 leaves, many of which are stained and soiled. Foll. 1 and 2 are much torn, and the former has been repaired with paper. The quires, signed with letters, are 21 in number, the last being imperfect. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 30 to 38 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, regular hand of the xith or xiith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The Magnificat of the eighth tone was written apparently by a later hand.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The hymn for Lent was written by a later hand.
Hand 4 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
