Description based on Wright DCCXC (2:781)
Date: 9th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Other, 19 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Cyril of Alexandria. Selections from the treatise of Cyril of Alexandria on Worship in Spirit and in Truth; Gregory the Monk. Writings of Gregory the Monk; Ammonius. Letters of Ammonius; Ephraim. Letter of Ephraim to the monks of the mountains. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 9 items.

Physical Description

Nineteen vellum leaves, about 11 1/2 in. by 7 3/8, most of which are more or less stained and mutilated (Add. 17,213, foll. 21—39). Each page is divided into two columns, of from 53 to 56 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is neat and regular, and seems to be of the ixth cent.
