Add. 17,261, foll. 1–8

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1075
Description based on Wright CCCCXLV (1:357)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Other, 8 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Severus. Hymns of Severus, etc., as arranged byJacob of Edessa. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 17 items.

Physical Description

Eight paper leaves, about 6 in. by 4 5/8, several of which are torn. There are from 17 to 20 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is neat and regular, of the xiiith cent.

Add. 17,261, foll. 9–66

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1076
Description based on Wright CCCCXLIV (1:358-359)
Date: 13th or 14th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 58 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Severus. Part of the Hymns of Severus; Supplicatory hymns, arranged according to the eight tones; A Calendar of Feasts and Saints' Days. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 6 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 6 7/8 in. by 5 1/8, consisting of 58 leaves (Add. 17,261, foll. 9—66), some of which are much soiled and torn, especially foll. 25, 26, 33, 34, 41, 48, 52, and 61—63. The quires are signed with letters, but many of them have been lost, and others are im¬perfect. At present the manuscript is defective at the beginning, and there are lacunae after foll. 16, 24, 34, 40, 48, 52, and 58. The number of lines in cach page varies from 12 to 19.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing, which is of the xiiith or xivth cent., is large and inelegant.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is different on foll. 63—66.

Contents

This manuscript contains 6 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Note: Imperfect at the beginning

Note: Imperfect

Note: Imperfect

Add. 17,261, foll. 67–140

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1077
Description based on Wright CCCXCVII (1:314)
Date: 16th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 74 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Services for the canonical hours of the ferial days. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 7 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 7 1/4 in. by 5 1/4, consisting of 74 leaves (Add. 17,261, foll. 67—140), some of which are much stained and slightly torn, especially foll. 69—72, 78, 80—83, and 140. The quires are without signatures, and the number of lines in each page varies from 16 to 20. This manuscript is imperfect at the beginning and elsewhere.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is very rude and inelegant, apparently of the xvith cent.

This manuscript contains 7 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.
