Add. 14,723, foll. 1, 2

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/608
Description based on Wright CCCCLXXIII (1:377)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Other, 2 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Hymns, numbered fromܒtoܙ. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Two paper leaves, about 6 in. by 4 5/8, the first of which is much torn, with 22 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): They belonged to a neatly written paper manuscript of the xiiith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Add. 14,723, foll. 3–65

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/609
Description based on Wright CCCCXLVIII (1:358)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 63 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Severus. The Hymns of Severus, etc., 300 (ܫ) in number, arranged according to the eight tones; Supplicatory hymns, arranged according to the eight tones. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 13 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 6 1/4 in. by 4 5/8, consisting of 63 leaves (Add. 14,723, foll. 3—65), some of which are slightly stained and torn, especially fol. 65. The quires are signed with letters, but several are lost, both at the beginning and the end, and there is also a lacuna after fol. 8. Each page has from 18 to 21 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular hand of the xiiith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 13 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Add. 14,723, foll. 66–113

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/610
Description based on Wright CCXVII (1:144-145)
Date: 13th century
West Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 48 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Psalters
This manuscript contains: The Psalms proper for the several daily services, according to the Peshitta version, accompanied by certain prayers. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 30 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 6 1/4 in. by 4 3/4, consisting of 48 leaves (Add. 14,723, foll. 66—113), some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 66—69, 86—94, 101—103, 108, 112, and 113. The quires, signed with letters, were at least 15 in number; but the first nine are altogether lost, and there is a lacuna after fol. 103. Each page has from 16 to 20 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, West Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a rather inelegant hand of the xiiith cent., with numerous Greek vowels.

This manuscript contains 30 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.
