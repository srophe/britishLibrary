Add. 17,253, foll. 1–69

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1046
Description based on Wright CCCCXXXVIII (1:352-353)
Date: AD 1222 / AG 1533
Origin: Convent of S. Mary Deipara
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 69 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Severus, John bar Aphtūnāyā. Part of the Hymns of Severus, John bar Aphtūnāyā; Supplicatory hymns, arranged according to the eight tones; Chants to be sung before the Lessons, arranged according to the eight tones; Hymns of praise, to be sung at the time of the celebration of the holy Eucharist, arranged according to the eight tones; A hymn in commemoration of Theodore; .... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 10 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 7 in. by 4 7/8, consisting of 69 leaves, some of which are slightly stained and torn, especially foll. 1—3, 7, 8, 10, 41, and. 42. The quires are signed with letters from ܝܕ to ܟܐ, and by a later hand from ܝ to ܝܙ. Consequently, the first 13 are lost; and there are also lacunae after foll. 1 and 9. The number of lines in each page varies from 15 to 26.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular hand; dated A. Gr. 1533, A.D. 1222.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Arabic script): See additions.

Add. 17,253, foll. 70–103

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1047
Description based on Wright CCCCLXXXIX (1:381)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 34 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: ܬܟܫ̈ܦܬܐ, arranged according to the eight tones. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Paper, about 7 in. by 4 7/8, consisting of 34 leaves (Add. 17,253, foLl. 70—103), a few of which are more or less torn, especially foil. 70, 75, and 103. The quires are signed with letters from ܐ to ܕ, and by later hands from ܟܒ to ܟܗ and from ܝܚ to ܟܐ. Leaves are wanting at the beginning and end, as well as after foll. 70, 74, 75, and 102. The number of lines in each page varies from 15 to 23.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a rather inelegant hand of the xiii cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 70a): ܬܟܫ̈ܦܬܐ, arranged according to the eight tones [defective]
