Add. 18,821

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1134
Description based on Wright DCCLXXXVI (2:775-776)
Date: 9th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 18 leaves.
Features: Decoration
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Gregory Nazianzen. Part of the poetical works and letters of Gregory Nazianzen.; A fragment of a selection from the Scholia or Commentary of Olympiodorus of Alexandria on the Organon of Aristotle. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 14 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 15 1/8 in. by 11 3/8, consisting of 18 leaves, some of which are much stained and mutilated, especially foll. 3, 5, 6, 9, 16, and 17. Leaves are wanting at the beginning and end, as well as after foll. 8, 9, 10, 12, 14, 15, and 16. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 40 to 49 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): The writing is a fine, regular Estrangela.
Hand 2 (minor, ): See additions.
Decoration: There are some slight attempts at ornament in this volume, e.g. foll. 4 a, 12 a, and 17 a.
