Add. 17,229, foll. 1–47

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1009
Description based on Wright CCLXIII (1:207-208)
Date: AD 1218
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 47 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Missals
This manuscript contains: A Collection of Anaphoras. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 9 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 1/8 in. by 6 5/8, consisting of 47 leaves. The quires appear to have been eight in number, but they are all more or less imperfect, leaves being wanting at the beginning, as well as after foll. 3, 11, 19, 25, 33, and 37. There are from 15 to 18 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular hand, dated A. Gr. 1529, A.D. 1218.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Contents

This manuscript contains 9 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1a): A Collection of Anaphoras [defective]
Item 2 (Fol. 1a): Julius, Pope of Rome. Anaphora of Julius of Rome [defective]
 
Title: ܐܢܢܦܘܪܐ ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܐܝܘܠܝܘܣ
Note: Very Imperfect
Item 3 (Fol. 2b): Philoxenus of Mabug. Anaphora of Philoxenus of Mabug [defective]
 
Title: ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܦܝܠܠܟܣܝܢܘܣ ܐܦܝܣܩܘܦܐ ܕܡܒܘܓ  Incipit: ܐܠܗܐ ܚ̣̈ܝܐ ܕܟܠ ܘܢܘܼܗܪܐ ܏ܘܫ  Incipit: ܐܠܗܐ ܚ̣̈ܝܐ ܕܟܠ ܘܢܘܼܗܪܐ . ܏ܘܫ
Note: See Renaudot, Liturg. Orient., t. ii., p. 301
Item 4 (Fol. 4b): Jacob of Batnae. Anaphora of Jacob of Batnae [defective]
 
Title: ܕܡܪܝ ܝܥܩܘܒ ܡܲܠܦܢܐ
Item 5 (Fol. 16b): Thomas of Heraclea. Anaphora of S. Thomas the Apostle (sic!)
 
Title: ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܬܐܘܡܐ ܫܠܝ̣ܚܐ
Note: It is the anaphora of Thomas of Heraclea (See Renaudot, t. ii., p. 383)
Item 6 (Fol. 20a): John the Evangelist. Anaphora of S. John the Evangelist [defective]
 
Title: ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܝܘܚܢܢ ܐܘܢܓܠܝܣܛܐ
Item 7 (Fol. 26a): James, Brother of our Lord. Anaphora of S. James, the brother of our Lord [defective]
Item 8 (Fol. 34a): Xystus of Rome. Anaphora of Xystus of Rome [defective]
 
Title: ܕܩܕ ܟܣܘܣܛܘܣ ܐܦܝ܏ܣ ܕܪܘܡܝ
Note: See Renaudot, t. ii., p. 134.
Item 9 (Fol. 37b): Lazarus bar Sabta. Anaphora of Lazarus bar Sabta, or Philoxenus, of Bagdad [defective]
 
Title: ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܠܥܙܪ ܒܪ ܣܒܬܐ ܚܣܝܐ ܕܒܓܕܕ  Colophon: See additions.

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 45b): In the colophon, fol. 45 b, the scribe, Abu 'l-Fadl, gives the date and a list of the contents, from which it appears that the Anaphora of Eustathius (see Renaudot, t. ii., p. 235) once stood at the commencement of the volume. ܫ̈ܠܡܝ ܗܠܝܢ ܬܫ̈ܥܐ ܐܢܢܦܘܪ̈ܐܣ ܐܘܟܝܬ ܩܘܪ̈ܒܐ ܩܕܡܝܐ ܐܘܣܛܐܬܝܘܣ: ܘܕܬܪ̈ܝܢ ܕ܏ܩܕ ܐܝܘܠܝܘܣ: ܘܕܬܠܬܐ ܦܝܠܠܝܟܣܝܢܘܣ: ܘܕܐܪ̈ܒܥܐ ܏ܕܩܕ ܡܪܝ ܝܥܩܘܒ ܡܲܠܦܢܐ: ܘܕܚܡܫܐ ܕ܏ܩܕ ܡܪܝ ܬܐܘܡܐ ܫܠܝܚܐ: ܘܕܐܫܬܐ ܕܝܘܚܢܢ ܐܘܢܓܠܝܣܛܐ: ܘܕܫܒ̈ܥܐ ܡܪܝ ܝܥܩܘܒ ܐܚܘܗܝ ܕܡܪܢ: ܘܕܬܡܢܝܐ ܟܣܘܣܛܘܣ ܕܪܘܡܝ: ܘܕܬܫܥܐ ܕܠܥܙܪ ܒܪ ܣܒܬܐ ܕܒܓܕܕ. ܐ܏ܫܬ ܒܫܢ̣ܬ݀ ܐܠܦ ܘܚܡ̈ܫܡܐܐ ܘ܏ܟܛ ܒܝܪܚܐ ܕܐܝܪ ܒܬܠܬܐ ܒܗ ܒܝܘܡ ܥܐܕܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܕܢܝܐܝܠ. ܟܠ ܕܩܪܐ ܢܨܠܐ ܥܠ ܚܛܝܐ ܐܒܘܠܦܕܠ ܕܣܪܛ.
Addition 2 (Fol. 46a): On fol. 46 a there is a prayer, ܨܠܘܬܐ ܕܡܬܐܡܪܐ ܒܝܘܡܐ ܕܚܡ̈ܫܐ ܕܪ̈ܐܙܐ ܚܠܦ ܗ̇ܝ ܕܫܠܡܐ ; which is followed by a doxology.
Addition 3 (Fol. 46b): On foll. 46 b and 47 a, there stands another prayer, to be used at the placing of the sacred elements on the altar.

Add. 17,229, foll. 48–77

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1010
Description based on Wright CCLXV (1:209)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 30 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Missals
This manuscript contains: A Small Collection of Anaphoras. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 4 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 1/8 in. by 6 5/8, consisting of 30 leaves (Add. 17,229, foll. 48—77). The quires, signed with letters, are three in number. There are from 17 to 20 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular hand of the xiiith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 4 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 48a): A Small Collection of Anaphoras
Item 2 (Fol. 48a): Anaphora of S. Mark
 
Title: ܐܢܢܦܘܪܐ ܕܛܘܼܒܢܐ ܡܪܩܘܣ ܐܘܢܓܠܝ̣ܣܛܐ
Note: See Renaudot, Liturg. Orient., t. ii., p. 176.
Item 3 (Fol. 56a): Philoxenus of Mabug. Anaphora of Philoxenus of Mabug
  Incipit: ܡܪܝܐ ܐܠܗܐ ܚ̇ܝ̣ܠܬܢܐ ܘܩܕܝܫܐ ܘܐܚ̣ܝܕ ܟܠ. ܏ܘܫ
Item 4 (Fol. 63b): Ignatius. Anaphora of Ignatius
 
Title: ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܡܪܝ ܐܝܓܢܐܛܝ̣ܘܣ ܢܘܪܢܐ

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 77b): At the foot of fol. 77 b we read: ܒ̇ܥܐ ܐܢ̣ܐ ܡܢ ܟܠ ܕܦ̇ܓܥ ܐܘܿ ܡܩܲܪܒ ܒܗܠܝܢ ܩܘܪ̈ܒܐ ܢܨ̇ܠܐ ܥܠ ܡ̇ܢ ܕܟܬ݂ܒ. ܘܗ̇ܘ ܕܡܨ̇ܠܐ ܗ̣ܘ ܢܬܚܲܣܐ.
