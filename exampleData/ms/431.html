Add. 14,663, foll. 1–22

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1475
Description based on Wright DCCLI (2:691-692)
Date: 6th or 7th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 22 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Cyril of Alexandria. The "Explanatio duodecim capitum" of Cyril of Alexandria , from the seventh anathema to the end; the orthodox bishops (Sergius, Marion, Nonnus, Thomas, and John). 2. A letter written by the orthodox bishops (Sergius, Marion, ܡܪܝܘܢ , Nonnus, ܢܘܢܐ , Thomas, and John) to the monks of Amid; Not improbably several treatises on the same subject; John Chrysostom. Fragments of several homilies of John Chrysostom. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 25 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 1/4 in. by 6 3/4, consisting of 22 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1 4—6, 12—15, 17—21, and 23. The quires are signed with letters, and were originally at least 22 in number (fol 7 a, ܛ; fol. 21 a, ܟܒ); but not a single one is now perfect. As at present bound, there are lacunae after foll. 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 10, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 18, and 22. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 34 to 38 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, current hand of the vith or viith cent.

Add. 14,663, fol. 23

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1476
Description based on Wright DCCLI (2:692)
Date: AD 815—912 / 3rd century, AH
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Leaf, 1 leaf.
Wright's Subject Classification: Fly Leaves
This manuscript contains: Severus of Antioch. Two short extracts from the writings of Severus of Antioch. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Whether the fly-leaf, fol. 23, which is much stained and torn, really belongs to this manuscript, is doubtful.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script):
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 23a): Severus of Antioch. Two short extracts from the writings of Severus of Antioch

Additions

N.B., folio location may not be available for some items below.
Addition 1 (Fol. 23b): On the recto, there are three entries of deaths. The first is that of a bishop. In the third, the words ܘܡܬܝܢ ܕܛܝܝ̈ܐ are still legible, showing that these entries belong to the third cent. of the Hijra, A.D. 815—912.
Addition 2: All three of the entries of deaths on the recto have been purposely effaced.
