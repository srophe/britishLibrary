Add. 17,194

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/850
Description based on Wright DCCCLXII (2:1002-1003)
Date: AD 886 / AG 1197
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 84 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Catenae Patrum and Demonstrations against Heresies
This manuscript contains: Demonstrations from the Fathers on various Biblical and theological subjects. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 4 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 5 1/4 in. by 3 1/2, consisting of 84 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1—7, 46, 52—54, 64, 81—84. The quires, signed with letters, are 8 in number. A leaf is wanting after fol. 74. There are from 19 to 25 lines in each page. This volume is dated A. Gr. 1197, A.D. 886.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a small, current hand.
Hand 2 (minor, ): See additions.

Additions

N.B., items below may have been listed out of folio order in Wright's catalogue.
Addition 1 (Fol. 82a): The colophon, fol. 82 a, informs us that the manuscript was written by one Ignatius of Mabug, A. Gr. 1197, A.D. 886. ܐܝܓܢܐܛܝ ܒܨܝܪܐ ܕܡܢ ܡܒܘܓ ܟܬܒ̣ ܫܢܬ ܐܠܦ ܘܩ܏ܨܙ: ܕܩܪܐ ܢܨ̇ܠܐ ܕܢܬܚ̣ܢܢ.
Addition 2 (Fol. 1a): On fol. 1 a there are couple of lines of Greek, in slanting uncials, now much effaced.
Addition 3 (Fol. 1a): On fol. 1 a there is a Syriac note, written by one Elisha, and dated A. Gr. 1268, A.D. 957: ܐܢܐ ܐܠܝܫܥ ܡܫܡܫܢܐ ܕܡܢ ܐܬܪܐ ܕܐ . . . ܕܟܬܒܬ ܪܘܫܡܐ ܗܢܐ ܒܗܪܝܘܡـ . . . ܒܫܢܬ ܐܠܦ ܘܡܐܬܝܢ ܘܫܬܝܢ ܘܬܡܢܐ . . .
Addition 4 (Fol. 82b): On fol. 82 b there is a list of the bishops who consecrated Severus of Antioch; and on fol. 83 a, a short biographical notice of that patriarch, These were copied by one Basil "from an ancient manuscript," in the year 1381, A.D. 1070. ܒܐܣܝܠܠܝܘܣ ܒܨܝܪܐ [ܟܬܒ] ܗܠܝܢ ܡܢ ܨܚܚܐ ܥܬܝܩܐ ܐܝܟ ܕܠܥܘܗܕܢܐ ܫ̇ܢܬ ܐܫ܏ܦܐ. ܕܡܪܝܐ ܢܚ̇ܣܐ ܠܕܡܚ̇ܣܐ ܠܢ..
Addition 5 (Fol. 83b): Fol. 83 b, which is much soiled and torn, contains an extract relative to fasting, and another apparently referring to the course of the sun.
