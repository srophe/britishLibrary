Add. 17,273

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1108
Description based on Wright CCCCXXXI (1:347-349)
Date: 11th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 114 leaves.
Features: Decoration
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: A ܬܟܫܦܬܐ of the third tone; A ܬܟܫܦܬܐ of the seventh tone; Severus, John bar Aphtūnāyā. The Hymns of Severus, John bar Aphtūnāyā, etc., in number 165 (ܬܣܗ); Severus, Ephraim, Jacob of Batnae. A collection of Prosphorici for the principal festivals, etc.; A hymn. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 34 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 7 1/2 in. by 5 7/8, consisting of 114 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, are 9 in number. A single leaf is wanting after fol. 113. There are from 21 to 30 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in an inelegant, though tolerably regular, current hand; it belongs to the xith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Decoration (Illustration): Salībā bar Zechariah S. George (ܩܕܝܫܐ ܡܪܝ ܓܘܪܓܝܣ) Domitius (ܩܕܝܫܐ ܡܪܝ ܕܝܡܛ) fol. 1 b
