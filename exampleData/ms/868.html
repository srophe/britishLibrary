Add. 17,206, upper text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1434
Description based on Wright DCCCXXXI (2:859-860)
Date: 11th or 12th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 88 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: John Chrysostom. An exposition of Ps. vi. by John Chrysostom.; Ephraim. Extracts from Ephraim.; Philoxenus of Mabūg. An extract from Philoxenus of Mabūg .; Jacob of Batnae. Extracts from Jacob of Batnae.; Ephraim. An alphabetical hymn of Ephraim, entitled, "of doctrine."; .... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 16 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 5 5/8 in. by 3 7/8, consisting of 88 leaves, many of which are much stained and soiled, and a few of them torn, especially foll. 1, 87, and 88. The quires have no signatures, and the first is imperfect, a leaf being wanting at the beginning. The number of lines in each page varies from 10 to 20. This manuscript is palimpsest throughout.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): A first of perhaps two hands, in an inelegant character of the xith or xiith cent.
Hand 2 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): A potential second hand, in an inelegant character of the xith or xiith cent.

Add. 17,206, foll. 1–42, lower text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1435
Description based on Wright DCCCXXXI (2:859-860)
Date: ca. 9th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 42 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Canons for various festivals, etc. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

This manuscript is palimpsest throughout, being written on portions of two more ancient manuscripts. The first of these, encompassing foll. 1—42 is from a manuscript of the same dimensions, consisting of five quires, the first three of which are signed with letters.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): Written in a small, neat hand of about the ixth cent.

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.
