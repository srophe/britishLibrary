Add. 14,638, foll. 1–18, upper text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/401
Description based on Wright DXXIII (1:398-399)
Date: 9th or 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 18 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Funeral Services
This manuscript contains: Consolatory discourses and prayers for the Dead; Short ejaculatory prayers on various occasions, for the whole people and for individuals; Consolatory phrases. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 14 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 6 3/4 in. by 4 3/4, consisting of 18 leaves, several of which are slightly stained and torn, especially foll. 11—13 and 18. The number of lines in each page varies from 26 to 37. This manuscript is palimpsest; cf. the lower text.

Hand 1 (minor, Estrangela script): The first two pages are written in Estrangela, of the ixth or xth cent.
Hand 2 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): The remainding pages (foll. 3-18) are in a more cursive character, also of the ixth or xth cent.

Add. 14,638, foll. 1–18, lower text

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1478
Description based on Wright DXXIII (1:399)
Date: 7th century
Unspecified Greek script ܀ Parchment Codex, 18 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Portions of a Greek Lectionary from the Gospels. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

This manuscript is palimpsest; cf. the upper text. Some of the rubrics are still quite legible; for example, foll. 1 a, 4 b, 6 b, 7 a, 8 b, 11 a, and 13 b. The text can be read with considerable ease in most places, but particularly on foll. 1 a and 18 b, which have been left blank by the Syrian scribe.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Greek script): Viiith cent., written in rather elegant, slanting uncials, with larger initial letters and with accents.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1a): Portions of a Greek Lectionary from the Gospels
 
Title: ܒܘ̈ܝܐܐ ܕܥܠ ܥܢܝ̈ܕܐ

Add. 14,638, foll. 19–26

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/402
Description based on Wright DXXIV (1:399)
Date: 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Other, 8 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Funeral Services
This manuscript contains: Consolatory discourses and prayers for the Dead. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 8 items.

Physical Description

Eight vellum leaves, 6 1/2 in. by 4 7/8, with from 18 to 28 lines in each page (Add. 14,638, foll. 19—26).

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is good and regular, of about the xth cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The character employed on fol. 23 b, etc., is more cursive than that of the previous pages.

Contents
