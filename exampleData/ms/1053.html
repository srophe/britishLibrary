Add. 17,257, foll. 1–21

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1054
Description based on Wright CCVII (1:141)
Date: AD 1248 / AG 1559
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Other, 21 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Psalters
This manuscript contains: Portions of both parts of a Choir-book, comprising Psalms xlv.—lxxxii., which were chanted before the altar at the celebration of the holy Eucharist. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 3 items.

Physical Description

Twenty-one paper leaves, about 6 1/2 in. by 4 7/8, most of which are more or less stained and torn. The quires are signed with letters. There are from 13 to 15 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is large and regular, and dated A. Gr. 1559, A.D. 1248.

Contents

This manuscript contains 3 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1a): Portions of both parts of a Choir-book, comprising Psalms xlv.—lxxxii., which were chanted before the altar at the celebration of the holy Eucharist.
Item 2 (Fol. 1a): The first part (ܐ)
Note: Of the first part there remain: Ps. xlviii. 5—xlix. 10; l. 23—lv. 12; lxi. 2— lxvi. 11; lxix. 17—lxxi. 15; lxxvi. 2— lxxvii. 10; and lxxx. 10—lxxxi. 11.
Item 3 (Fol. 12b): The second part (ܒ)
 
Title: ܡܙܡܘܪ̈ܐ ܕܦܠܓܗ ܕܝܘܡܐ
Note: Of it there remain: Ps.xlv. 1—xlvi. 5; xlviii. 3—lvi. 9; and lxxxii. 6—8.

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 21a): On fol. 21 a there is a note, giving the date, and saying that this manuscript was written by a "feeble old man," aided by Rabban Habīb. The scribe seems therefore to have been the same Bacchus who wrote Add. 17,256 . ܫܠܡ̣ܘ ܐܝܟ ܒܟܬܐ ܕܕܝܪ̈ܝܐ ܩܕܝ̈ܫܐ ܕܒܕܝܪܐ ܕܣܘܪ̈ܝܝܐ ܡܙܡܘܪ̈ܐ ܕܡܙܡܪܝܢ ܩܕܡ ܡܕܒܚܐ ܒܥܕܢܐ ܕܩܘܪܒܐ. ܕܩ̇ܪܐ ܢܨ̇ܠܐ ܥܠ ܣܒܐ ܡܚ̣ܝܠܐ ܕܣ̣ܪܛ ܘܥܠ ܪܒܢ ܚܒܝܒ ܕܥ̇ܕܪ. ܘܥܠ ܟܠ ܕܐܫܬܘܬܦ. ܗ̈ܘܝ ܗܠܝܢ ܫܢܬ ܐܠܦ ܘܢ̇܏ܢܛ ܕܝ̈ܘܢܝܐ ܢܟܝ̈ܠܐ.

Add. 17,257, foll. 22–73

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1055
Description based on Wright CCXIV (1:143-144)
Date: 13th or 14th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 52 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Psalters
This manuscript contains: Large portions of the second part of a Choir-book, containing the Psalms, according; to the Peshitta version. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 5 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 6 7/8 in. by 5 1/4, consisting of 52 leaves (Add. 17,257, foll. 22—73), most of which, are more or less stained and torn. The quires are signed with letters. The number of lines in each page varies from 11 to 15.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a rather inelegant hand of the xiiith or xivth cent., with a few Greek vowels.

Contents

This manuscript contains 5 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 22a): Large portions of the second part of a Choir-book, containing the Psalms, according; to the Peshitta version
Notes:
  • We have here: part of Ps. xvii.; xxiv. 4—xxvi.12; xxviii. 2— xxxi. 7; xxxii. 2—xxxiv. 2; xxxv. 20— xxxvii. 20; xii. 4—xliv. 2; xiv. 13—xlvii. 1; xlviii. 13—xlix. 18; li. 15—liii. 1; lv. 9— lvi. 2; lix. 5—Ixii. 10; lxv. 1—lxviii. 4; lxviii. 26—lxix. 34; lxxi. 12—xcv. 3; xcviii. 9—cii. 22; civ. 24—cv. 12; cviii. 2—cxviii. 24; and cxviii. 61—87
  • The ܡܪ̈ܡܝܬܐ and ܫܘܒ̈ܚܐ are marked, and the verses divided by ܗ. The commencements of the various daily services are also rubricated in the text.
Item 2 (Fol. 23b): Part a
 
Title: ܬܫܡ̣ܫܬܐ ܕܬܠܬ ܫ̈ܥܝܢ ܕܟܠܝܘܡ
Item 3 (Fol. 40b): Part b
 
Title: ܕܬܫ̈ܥ ܫ̈ܥܝܢ
Item 4 (Fol. 53b): Part c
 
Title: ܕܪܡܫܐ ܕܟܠܝܘܡ
Item 5 (Fol. 72a): Part d
 
Title: ܕܬܪ̈ܬܝܢ ܕܠܠܝܐ
