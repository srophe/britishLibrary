Add. 14,588

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/324
Description based on Wright DCCCIII (2:806-807)
Date: 9th or 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 150 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Ephraim. A metrical discourse of Ephraim; Jacob of Batnae. Metrical discourses of Jacob of Batnae. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 18 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 in. by 6 7/8, consisting of 150 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 41, 101, 119, 120, 131, 136, 138, 143, and 150. The quires, signed with letters, were at least 20 in number, but of these the first is lost, and most of those after ܝܒ are very imperfect. Leaves are wanting after foll. 119, 120, 130, 137, 140,142, and 144. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 21 to 29 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, regular hand of the ixth or xth cent.
