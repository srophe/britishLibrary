Add. 17,189, foll. 1–16

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/844
Description based on Wright DXXXIV (2:407)
Date: 5th or 6th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 16 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: St. Ephraim the Syrian. Homilies on different subjects by Ephraim. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 6 items.

Physical Description

Several of the leaves are much stained and torn, especially foll.1, 7, and 16

Leaves are wanting after foll. 6, 7, 8, 9, 14, 15, and 16.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): This manuscript is written in a fine, Edessene hand of the vth or vith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Contents

This manuscript contains 6 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1a): St. Ephraim the Syrian. Homilies on different subjects by Ephraim [defective]
 
Title: See additions.
Note: Cf. S. Ephraemi Syri etc. Opera Selecta, ed. Overbeck, p. 74.; Overbeck, p. 81.; Overbeck, p. 88.; Overbeck, p. 95.; Overbeck, p. 99.
Item 2 (Fol. 1b): Ephrem. On the Miracles wrought by Moses in Egypt.
 
Title: ܡܐܡܪܐ ܩܕܡܝܐ ܥܠ ܐܬ̈ܘܬܐ ܕܥܒ̣ܕ ܡܘܫܐ ܒܡܨܪܝܢ
Notes:
  • See S. Ephraemi Syri etc. Opera Selecta, ed. Overbeck, p. 88.
  • Cf. S. Ephraemi Syri etc. Opera Selecta, ed. Overbeck, p. 88.
Item 3 (Fol. 4b): Ephrem. On the coming of the Spirit and the Dividing of the Tongues in the Upper Chamber (Acts ch. ii.)
 
Title: ܥܠ ܡܐܬܝܐ ܕܪܘܚܐ ܘܦܘܠܓܐ ܕܠܫ̈ܢܐ ܒܥܠܝܬܐ
Notes:

Notes:
  • Imperfect. See Overbeck, p. 81.
  • Cf. Overbeck, p. 81.

Additions

N.B., folio location may not be available for some items below.
Addition 1: The title, fol.1b, has been effaced, and in its place we now read the following mutilated words, written by a later hand:ܕܡـ[ܪܝ ܐ]ܦܪܝܡ . . . ܫܪܝܖ(sic)ܐܠܐ ܠܒܣܝܠܠܝܣ ܐ̣ܘ ܠܝܘܐܢܢܝܣ; which seem to imply that the writer ascribed these homilies, not to Ephraim, but to Basil or John Chrysostom. However, on fol.9a we can still read the partially effaced running title ܕܡܪܝ ܐܦܪܝܡ; and again, on foll.12b and 13a, ܬܘܪ̈ܓܡܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܐܦܪܝܡ; besides (sic)ܕܡܪܝ ܦܪܝܡ on the margin of fol.2a in a later hand.
Addition 2 (Fol. 1a): On fol. 1a. there is a long note in the usual form, stating that this was one of the 250 volumes, which were brought to the convent of S. Mary Deipara by the abbat Moses of Nisibis, A Gr. 1243, A.D. 932.

Add. 17,189, foll. 17–21

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/845
Description based on Wright DCCCLXV (2:1016)
Date: 6th century
Estrangela script ܀ Parchment Other, 5 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Anonymous Works
This manuscript contains: Fragments of a Commentary on the book of Genesis, relating to portions of chapters iii., iv., vi., and viii. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Five vellum leaves, about 9 in. by 5 1/2, all much stained and torn, with from 32 to 34 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Estrangela script): The writing is a neat, regular Estrangělā of the vith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 17): Fragments of a Commentary on the book of Genesis, relating to portions of chapters iii., iv., vi., and viii.
Note: The following is one of the more legible passages; it treats of ch. vi., 3:ܗ̇ܝ ܕܝ̣ܢ ܕܢܗܘܘܢ ܝܘܡ̈ܬܗܘܢ ܡܐܐ ܘܥܣܪ̈ܝܢ ܫ̈ܢܝ̣ܢ. ܦܘܫܟܐ ܚܕܬܐ ܥܒܕܬ ܠܐ̈ܢܫܝܢ ܒܕܐܣܒܪܘ ܕܡܐܐ ܫ̈ܢܝܢ ܗܘܝ ܡܢ ܙܒܢܐ ܕܓܙܪ ܕܝܢ̣ܐ ܥܕܡܐ ܠܛܘܦܢܐ. ܘܐܬܕܡܪܘ ܕܐܝܟܢܐ ܡܐܐ ܘܥܣܪ̈ܝܢ ܫ̈ܢܝܢ ܡܬܚܙܐ ܕܐܡ̣ܪ ܐܠܗܐ. ܟܕ ܘ̇ܠܐ ܗܘܐ ܠܗܘܢ ܠܘܩܕܡ ܠܡܬܒܝܢܘ ܒܗܕܐ. ܕܐܠܘ ܡܢ ܒܬܪ ܡܐܐ ܘܥܣܪ̈ܝܢ ܫ̈ܢܝܢ ܨ̇ܒܐ ܗܘܐ ܐܠܗܐ ܕܢܝܬܐ ܡܡܘܠܐ̣. ܠܘ ܒܚܡܬܐ ܐܘ ܒܬܘܬܐ ܡ̇ܒܨܪ ܗܘܐ ܡܢ ܬܢܘܝ ܕܣܡ. ܘܐܠܘ ܬܘܒ ܡܢ ܒܬܪ ܡܐܐ ܫ̈ܢܝܢ ܨ̇ܒܐ ܗܘܐ ܠܡܝܬܝܘܬܗ̣. ܠܘ ܡܢ ܡܕܡ ܡܬܥܨܐ ܗ̇ܘܐ ܠܡܐܡܪ ܝܬܝܪ ܡܢ ܡܐ ܕܒ̇ܥܐ ܗ̇ܘܐ. ܟܕ ܐܦ ܗܕܐ ܝܕܝܥܐ ܗܝ ܕܐܠܗܐ ܠܐ ܡܥܕ ܕܝܬܝܪ ܡܢ ܠܘܚܡ̈ܘܗܝ ܢܣܪܚ ܓܙܪ ܕܝܢܘ̈ܗܝ. ܏ܘܫ.
