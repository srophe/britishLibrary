Add. 17,201, foll. 1–15

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/860
Description based on Wright DCCXLIX (2:690)
Date: 6th or 7th century
Estrangela script ܀ Parchment Other, 15 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Didymus of Alexandria. Part of the chapters of Didymus of Alexandria against the Arians, from the 16th to the 26th.; Cyril of Alexandria. The twelve chapters of Cyril of Alexandria against the Nestorians; Cyril of Alexandria. The confession of faith of Cyril of Alexandria; Philoxenus of Mabūg. The confession of faith of Philoxenus of Mabūg; Part of a theologico-philosophical treatise.; ... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 9 items.

Physical Description

Fifteen vellum leaves, about 9 ¼ in. by 5 7/8, some of which are slightly soiled and injured. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 27 to 31 lines. There are lacunae after foll. 6 and 9.

Hand 1 (sole, Estrangela script): The writing is a neat, regular Estrangela of the vith or viith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 9 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

  Subscription (Fol. 2b): ܫܠܡܘ ܩ̈ܦܠܐܐ ܕܐܡܪܝܢ (sic) ܠܕܝܕܡܘܣ ܐܠܟܣܢܕܪܝܐ ܠܘܩܒܠ ܐܪ̈ܝܢܘ.
Item 2 (Fol. 2b): Cyril of Alexandria. The twelve chapters of Cyril of Alexandria against the Nestorians
 
Title: ܪ̈ܫܐ ܬܪ̈ܥܣܪ ܕܗܝܡܝܘܬܐ ܕܐܡܪܝܢ (sic)ܠܘܩܒܠ ܗܪ̈ܣܝܘܛܐ ܕܒܝܬ ܢܣܛܘܪܝܣ. ܠܩܕܝܫܐ ܩܘܪܝܠܘܣ ܪܫܐ ܕܐܦܣ̈ܩܦܐ ܕܐܠܟܣܢܕܪܝܐ.
Item 3 (Fol. 4b): Cyril of Alexandria. The confession of faith of Cyril of Alexandria
 
Title: ܬ̣ܘܒ ܗܝܡܢܘܬܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܩܘܪܝܠܘܣ ܪܫܐ ܕܐ̇ܦܣ̈ܩܦܐ ܕܐܠܟܣܢܕܪܝܐ.   Incipit: ܩܘܪܝܠܘܣ ܏ܐܡ̇ܪ. ܡܗܝ̇ܡܢ ܐܢܐ ܒܐܒܐ ܘܒܒܪܐ̣ ܘܒܪܘܚܐ ܕܩܘܕܫܐ. ܒܐܠܗܐ ܗ̇ܘ ܕܐܫܬܕܪ ܡܢ ܐܠܗܐ. ܒܨܒܝܢܐ ܕܐܒܘܗܝ ܘܒܨܒܝܢܐ ܕܝܠܗ̣. ܘܒܨܒܝܢܐ ܕܪܘܚܐ ܕܩܘܕܫܐ. ܏ܘܫ.

Title: ܬܘܒ ܗܝܡܢܘܬܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܐܟܣܢܝܐ   Incipit: ܡܗܝܡܢܝܢ ܚܢܢ ܒܬܠܝܬܝܘܬܐ̣ ܕܚܕ ܟܝܢܐ ܡܬܘܡܝܐ. ܕܐܒܐ ܘܕܒܪܐ̣ ܘܕܪܘܚܐ ܕܩܘܕܫܐ. ܗ̇ܘ ܕܠܝܬ ܒܗ ܩܢܘܡܐ ܕܛܠ̣ܐ ܘܕܩܫܝܫ. ܘܠܐ ܨܒܝܢܐ ܦܠܝܓܐ. ܏ܘܫ.
Note: Imperfect at the end.

Item 7 (Fol. 8b): Another section

Item 8 (Fol. 8b): A third section
 
Title: ܐܚܪ̈ܢܝܬܐ   Incipit: ܡܢܗܝܢ ܕܝܢ ܕܐܘܣ̈ܝܘܣ ܐܝܬܝܗܝܢ ܡܪ̈ܟܒܬܐ̣ ܘܡܢܗܝܢ ܦܫܝ̈ܛܬܐ. ܏ܘܫ.   Subscription (Fol. 14a): ܫܠܡ ܡܡܠܠܐ ܕܐܒ̈ܗܬܐ ܩܕܝ̈ܫܐ ܕܥܠ ܗܝܡܢܘܬܐ.
Item 9 (Fol. 14a): Philoxenus of Mabūg. Part of the twelve chapters of Philoxenus of Mabūg [defective]
 
Title: ܪ̈ܫܐ ܬܪ̈ܥܣܪ ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܡܪܝ ܐܟܣܢܝܐ   Incipit: ܬܪ̈ܬܝܢ ܫܟ̈ܝܚܢ ܒܡܫܝܚܐ. ܕܒܬܪ̈ܝܢ ܓܒ̈ܝܢ̣. ܟܠ ܚܕܐ ܡܢܗܝ̣ܢ ܓܡܝܪܬܐ. ܘܗ̣ܘ ܒܟܠ ܚܕܐ ܡܢܗܝ̣ܢ ܡܫ̇ܠܡܢܐ.
Note: From the first to the third chapter, which is imperfect.

Add. 17,201, foll. 16–21

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/861
Description based on Wright DLXXIV (2:458)
Date: 8th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Other, 6 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: John the Monk. The latter half of the fourth discourse, of John the Monk, addressed to Eusebius and Eutropius. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Six vellum leaves, about 9 ½ in. by 6 ½ . The writing is good and regular, of the viiith cent., with from 25 to 32 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is good and regular, of the viiith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 16a): John the Monk. The latter half of the fourth discourse, of John the Monk, addressed to Eusebius and Eutropius.
  Subscription (Fol. 21b): ܫܠܡ ܡܐܡܪܐ ܕܐܪ̈ܒܥܐ: ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܡܪܝ ܝܘܚܢܢ ܝܚܝܕܝܐ ܒܚܝܪܐ ܕܠܩܘܫܬܝܢ: ܕܟܬܒܗ ܠܘܬ ܐܘܣܒܝܣ ܕܝܪܝܐ: ܒܫܘܐܠܐ ܘܒܦܘܢܝ ܦܬܓܡܐ܀
