Add. 17,158, foll. 1–48

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/791
Description based on Wright DCCXLVI (2:681-683)
Date: 6th or 7th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 48 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Jacob of Batnae. Metrical discourses and hymns of Jacob of Batnae; Isaac of Antioch. Metrical discourses of Isaac of Antioch; An index to the contents of the volume. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 15 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 5/8 in. by 6 ½, consisting of 48 leaves, many of which are stained by water, and some of them much torn, espeicially foll. 1—10. The quires, apparently signed with letters, were originally six in number, but the first is now lost. Each page has two columns, of from 31 to 36 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): The writing is a fine, regular Estrangela of the vith or viith cent.

Physical Description

Eight vellum leaves (Add. 17,158, foll. 49—56), 9 3/4 in. by 6 1/2, each page divided into two columns, of from 25 to 27 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): These leaves are written in a good, regular hand of about the viiith cent.
