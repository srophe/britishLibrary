Add. 14,670, fol. 1

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/511
Description based on Wright DXCIX (2:480)
Date: 6th or 7th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Leaf, 1 leaf.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: John Chrysostom. The commencement of the first discourse of John Chrysostom toTheodore. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

A vellum leaf, about 8 3/8 in. by 5 3/8, much mutilated. The writing is a small, neat Estrangělā of the vith or viith cent.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): The writing is a small, neat Estrangělā of the vith or viith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Arabic script): See additions.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1): John Chrysostom. The commencement of the first discourse of John Chrysostom to Theodore [defective]
 
Title: ܡܐܡܪܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܝܘܚܢܝܣ. ܐܦܣܩܘܦܐ ܕܩܘܣܛܢܛܝܢܦܘܠܘܣ. ܕܥܠ ܬܝܒܘܬܐ. ܕܒܗ̇ ܐܪܬܝ ܠܡ̇ܢ ܕܢܦ̣ܠ ܡܢ ܩܝܡܗ.
Note: Cf. Ad Theodorum lapsum, Opera, t. i., p. 1; Migne, Patrol. Gr., t, xlvii., col. 277

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 1b): On the recto there is some ancient Arabic writing, now much effaced.
