Add. 14,523, foll. 1–7

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/223
Description based on Wright CCLVI (1:204)
Date: 8th or 9th century
Estrangela script ܀ Parchment Other, 7 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Missals
This manuscript contains: Fragments of the Anaphora of S. James, the brother of our Lord. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Seven vellum leaves, 6 1/2 in. by 4 3/4, two of which are much torn. The writing is a neat, regular Estrangela of the viiith or ixth cent., with from 19 to 22 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Estrangela script): The writing is a neat, regular Estrangela of the viiith or ixth cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1): Fragments of the Anaphora of S. James, the brother of our Lord [defective]
Note: See Renaudot, Liturg. Orient., t. ii., p. 29; Assemani, Codex Liturgicus Universae Ecclesiae, t. v., p. 131

Add. 14,523, foll. 14–35

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/227
Description based on Wright CCCXLIX (1:293)
Date: 9th or 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Other, 22 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Greek canons for the Resurrection arranged according to the eight tones. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Twenty-two vellum leaves, about 6 1/2 in. by 4 ¾, belonging to a neatly written manuscript of the ixth or xth cent. The quires are signed with letters, and there are from 17 to 21 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): Neatly written.
