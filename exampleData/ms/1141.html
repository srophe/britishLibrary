Add. 23,596

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1141
Description based on Wright DCCXXIV (2:626-628)
Date: AD 1720 / AG 2031
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 422 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Individual Authors
This manuscript contains: Gregory bar Hebraeus.. The ܐܘܨܪ ܐܪ̈ܙܐ, or "Horreum Mysteriorum," of Gregory bar Hebraeus.; a list of the works of Gregory bar Hebraeus, with a few particulars regarding his life; . In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 36 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 12 3/8 in. by 8 1/4, consisting of 422 leaves. The quires, signed with letters as far as ܠܐ, fol. 308 b, are 42 in number. There are from 23 to 35 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, regular hand, with many Greek and Syriac vowels.
