Add. 14,526, foll. 1–39

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/250
Description based on Wright DCCCCVII (2:1033-1036)
Date: 7th century, probably soon after AD 641
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 39 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Councils of the Church and Ecclesiastical Canons
This manuscript contains: A Collection of Ecclesiastical Canons and Creeds. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 35 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 5/8 in. by 6, consisting of 39 leaves, the last of which is soiled and torn. The quires, four in number, are signed with letters, from ܟܗ to ܟܚ, but there was an older numeration, apparently from ܟܘ to ܟܛ (see fol. 31 a). Each page is divided into two columns, of from 33 to 43 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a small, neat hand of the viith cent., probably soon after A.D. 641 (see the List of the Emperors of Constantinople, beginning with Constantine the Great, and ending with Constantine the son of Heraclius, A.D. 641).
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Add. 14,526, foll. 40–47

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/251
Description based on Wright DCCCCVIII (2:1036)
Date: AD 641
Estrangela script ܀ Parchment Codex, 8 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Councils of the Church and Ecclesiastical Canons
This manuscript contains: A Synopsis of the Canons of the Apostles and the principal Councils, arranged under 51 heads; The Canons of the Apostles, as far as ܢܛ. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 2 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 5/8 in. by 6, consisting of 8 leaves (Add. 14,526, foll. 40—47), the first two of which are much stained and torn. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 33 to 38 lines. The contents are identical with a portion of those of the preceding number.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): The writing is a fine, regular Estrangělā of the viiith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 2 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 40b): A Synopsis of the Canons of the Apostles and the principal Councils, arranged under 51 heads
Note: The prefatory section is omitted.
Item 2 (Fol. 44b): The Canons of the Apostles, as far as ܢܛ

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 40a): Fol. 40 a seems originally to have contained some chronological notes, for we can still read near the top the words ܘܒܫܢܬ ܫܒܥܡܐܐ ܘܚܡܫ, and lower down the words ܡܢ ܥܝܕܬܐ (sic) ܕܐܘܪܗܝ . ܒܪܕܘܦܝܐ . . . ܫܬܡܐܐ ܘܬܡܢܝܢ ܘܬܫܥ . . .
Addition 2 (Fol. 40a): The entries on fol. 40 a have, however, been erased, and some words in Karshūnī written over them, of which the half has been torn away.
