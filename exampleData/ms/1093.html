Add. 17,269, foll. 1–38

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1094
Description based on Wright CCCCXCIII (1:382)
Date: AD 1499 / AG 1810
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 38 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Supplicatory hymns; Cantus ad Magnificat, arranged according to the eight tones; Jacob of Batnae. Prayers of Jacob of Batnae; Ephraim. Prayers of Ephraim. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 4 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 6 1/8 in. by 4 1/4, consisting of 38 leaves, one of which (fol. 6) is slightly torn. The quires are signed with letters (ܟܒ, ܟܓ, ܟܕ). There are lacunae after foll. 8 and 34. The number of lines in each page is 14 or 15.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a good, regular hand, with occasional Greek and Syriac vowels; dated A. Gr. 1810, A.D. 1499; written by the priest Moses of Mount Lebanon.

Contents

This manuscript contains 4 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1a): Supplicatory hymns [defective]
 
Title: ܬܟܫ̈ܦܬܐ   Subscription (Fol. 11a): ܫܠܡ ܬܟܫ̈ܦܬܐ ܩܦܝܣܐܝܬ: ܒܥܘܕܪܢܐ ܘܣ̣ܘܝ̇ܥܐ ܕܬܠܝܬܝܘܬܐ ܏ܩܕ: ܒܐܝ̈ܕܝ ܚܲܛܝܐ ܡܘܫܐ ܒܫܡ ܏ܩܫ ܘܕܝܪܝܐ. ܛܘܪܝܐ ܠܒܢܢܝܐ ܫܢ̣ܬ ܏ܐܦܝ ܕܝܘ̈ܢܝܐ ܕܩ̇ܪܐ ܢܨ̇ܠܐ ܘܢܬܕܟܪ ܠܚܲܛܝܐ ܥܲܗܝܕܐ ܕܣܪܛ.
Note: Of the first tone, only a small portion remains, and the seventh and fourth are slightly imperfect. The subscription on fol. 11 a gives the name of the scribe, the priest Moses of Mount Lebanon, and the date.
Item 2 (Fol. 11b): Cantus ad Magnificat, arranged according to the eight tones
 
Title: ܬܘܒ ܟ̇ܬܒܝܢܢ ܡܢܬܐ ܩܠܝܠ ܡܢ ܡܲܘܪ̈ܒܐ ܓܲܘ̈ܢܝܬܐ ܒܬܡ̈ܝܐ ܐܪ̈ܟܐܕܝܐܣ
Item 3 (Fol. 23b): Jacob of Batnae. Prayers of Jacob of Batnae [defective]
 
Title: ܒܫܡ ܬܠܝܬܝܘܬܐ ܡܫܲܒܚܬܐ ܘܣܓܝ̣ܕܬܐ: ܟ̇ܬܒܝܢܢ ܩܲܠܝܠ ܒܥ̈ܘܬܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܝܥܩܘܒ ܡܲܠܦܢܐ

Note: Imperfect at the end.

Add. 17,269, foll. 39–80

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1095
Description based on Wright CCCCXLIV (1:357)
Date: 13th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Codex, 42 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Hymns
This manuscript contains: Severus. Part of the Hymns of Severus; Supplicatory hymns arranged according to the eight tones. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 8 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 5 1/4 in. by 4, consisting of 42 leaves (Add. 17,269, foll. 39—80). Foll. 41—53 are stained with oil, and fol. 47 is much torn. The quires, signed with letters, were once at least 19 in number. Leaves are now wanting at the beginning and end, as well as after foll. 39, 40, 47, 53, 54, and 62. There are from 13 to 15 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a neat, regular hand of the xiiith cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 8 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 39a): Severus. Part of the Hymns of Severus [defective]
Item 2 (Fol. 39a): Severus. The Epiphany [defective]
Item 3 (Fol. 41b): Severus. S. Simeon the Aged
Item 4 (Fol. 42b): Severus. Cana of Galilee [defective]
 
Title: ܛܟܣܐ ܕܥܠ ܩܛܢܐ
Item 5 (Fol. 44a): Severus. Lent [defective]
Note: Imperfect
Item 6 (Fol. 48a): Severus. The Miracles [defective]
Note: Imperfect
Item 7 (Fol. 54a): Severus. The Praises of the blessed Virgin [defective]
Note: Imperfect
Item 8 (Fol. 55a): Supplicatory hymns arranged according to the eight tones [defective]
 
Title: ܬܟܫ̈ܦܬܐ
Note: Imperfect
