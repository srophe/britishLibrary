Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: A large collection of letters. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 45 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9 1/4 in. by 6, consisting of 127 leaves, a few of which are much stained and torn, especially the first and last. The quires, signed with letters, are 13 in number. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 32 to 49 lines. Some discourses of Ephraim were at one time bound with this volume.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a small, elegant hand of the vith or viith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Additions

N.B., folio location may not be available for some items below.
Addition 1: The colophon is unfortunately torn, and the name of the convent where it was written having been erased, we have only the name of the abbat left, Elias the Galilaean. ܐܫܬ݀ܠܡ ܟܬܒܐ ܗܢܐ [ܒܕܝܪܐ] ܩܕܝܫܬܐ ܕܒܝܬ . . . ܒܝܘ̈ܡܝ [ܚܣܝܐ] ܘܪܚ̇ܡ ܐܠܗܐ [ܪܝܫܕܝܪܐ ܡܪܝ] ܐܠܝܐ ܓܠܝܠܝܐ܀ ܟܠ ܕܝܢ ܕܩ̇ܪܐ ܒـ[ـܟܬܒܐ ܗܢܐ ܢܨܠܐ] ܚܠܦܝ ܒܨܝܪܐ ܘ . . . ܕܢܬܚܢܢ ܒܝܘܡ [ܕܝܢܐ ܐܝܟ] ܓܝܣܐ ܕܡܢ [ܝܡܝܢܐ ܐܡܝܢ.]
Addition 2 (Fol. 1a): A note on fol. 1 a, in the usual form, states that this was one of the 250 manuscripts, brought by the abbat Moses of Nisibis to the convent of S. Mary Deipara, A. Gr. 1243, A.D. 932.Underneath this, the following words are written three times, with very slight variations: ܟܘܪܣܐ ܩܕܡܝܬܐ ܕܐܝܩܪܐ ܫܦܝܪܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܣܐܘܪܐ ܦܛܪܝܪܟܐ ܕܐܢܛܝܘܟܝܐ ܡܕܝܢܬܐ܀
Addition 3 (Fol. 1a): Still lower down we find, in a different hand, the name of one John: ܘܥܠ ܝܘܚܢܢ ܬܚܘܒܐ ܕܟܬ̣ܒ ܥܘܗܕܢܐ ܗܢܐ ܢܗܘܘܢ ܥܠܘܗܝ ܪ̈ܚܡܐ ܒܬܪ̈ܝܗܘܢ ܥ̈ܠܡܐ܀
Addition 4 (Fol. 1b): The note on the margin of fol. 1 b shows that some discourses of Ephraim were at one time bound with this volume.
