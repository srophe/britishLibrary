Add. 17,190

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/846
Description based on Wright CCCVII (1:243-244)
Date: AD 893 / AG 1204
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 123 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: The second volume of a collection of Choral Services and Homilies for the principal festivals of the whole year. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 42 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 1/2 in. by 7 1/8, consisting of 123 leaves, many of which, are much stained, defaced, and torn, especially foll. 1, 7, 17, 27—29, 73, 74, 77—79, 88, 112, 117, and 119—123. The quires, signed with letters, were once 30 in number, but ܝܓ to ܟܙ are now wholly lost, and several of the others are very imperfect, there being lacunae after foll. 1, 3, 4, 72, 73, 77, 78, 109, 110, 113, 114, 115, and 117. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 27 to 36 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a good, current hand, dated A. Gr. 1204, A.D. 893 (see Add. 14,515 ).

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 1a): Fol. 1 a exhibits an index to the discourses contained in the volume. A note at the end of this index gives the name of the scribe, John. ܟܠ ܕܩܪܐ ܢܨ̇ܠܐ ܝܘܚܢܢ (sic) ܕܘܝܐ ܕܣ̣ܪܛ ܕܢܬܚܢ̣ܢ ܐܝܟ ܓܝܣܐ ܒܝܘܡܐ ܕܕܝܢ̣ܐ ܐܡܝܢ.
Addition 2 (Fol. 123b): On fol. 123 b, there is a note, similar to that in Add. 14,515, 311b . ܠܐܝܩـ[ܪܐ] [ܘܠܬܫ]ܒܘܚܬܐ ܕ[ܬܠܝܬܝܘܬܐ] ܩܕܝܫܬܐ. ܐܬܚ[ܦܛ] ܘܣ̣ܡ ܣܝܡܬܐ ܗܕܐ ܪܘܚ[ܢܝـ]ܬܐ. ܡܢ ܡܕܡ ܕܙܢܬܗ ܛܝܒܘܬܐ ܐܠܗܝܬܐ. ܘܡܢ ܦܘܠܚܢܐ ܕܐܝܕ̈ܘܗܝ. ܝܘܚܢܢ ܚܛܝܐ ܡܢ ܩܪܩ[ܣܝـ]ـܘܢ ܡܕܝܢܬܐ. ܡܢ ܕܝܪܐ ܕܡܪܝ ܝܘܠܝܢܐ ܣܗܕܐ ܒܪܝܟܐ. ܕܐܠܗܐ ܗ̇ܘ ܕܡܛܠ ܫܡܗ ܩܕܝܫܐ. ܝ̣ܨܦ ܘܟܬܒ̣ ܦܠܓܘܬܐ ܗܕܐ ܕܬܪ̈ܬܝܢ ܕܚܘܕܪܐ. ܗ̣ܘ ܢܚܣܐ ܠܗ ܚܛܗ̈ܘܗܝ ܏ܘܫ. ܟܠ ܕܩ̇ܪܐ ܢܨ̇ܠܐ ܥܠ ܝܘܚܢܢ ܚܛܝܐ ܘܥܠ ܟܠ ܕܐܫܬܘܬܦ ܐܢ ܒܡܠܬܐ ܘܐܢ ܒܥܒܕܐ ܐܡܝܢ.
