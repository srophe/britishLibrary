Add. 14,552

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/283
Description based on Wright DCXII (2:485-486)
Date: 7th or 8th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 150 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification:
This manuscript contains: Cyril of Alexandria. Homilies of Cyril of Alexandria on the Gospel of St. Luke. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 3 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 11 1/2 in. by 7 1/4, consisting of 150 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 17, 26, 43, 44, 54, 61, 63 and 94. The quires, signed with letters, must originally have been 18 or 19 in number; but the last two or three are lost, and several of the others are imperfect, leaves being wanting after foll. 6, 7, 39, 73 and 94. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 36 to 44 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This volume is written in a fine, flowing hand of the viith or viiith cent. See additions.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

Manuscript Contents

Note: Item numbering updated in digital edition.
Item 1 (Fol. 1b): Cyril of Alexandria. Homilies of Cyril of Alexandria on the Gospel of St. Luke [defective]
Item 2 (Fol. 1b): An index prefixed to the volume
 
Title: ܗܠܝܢ ܬܘܪ̈ܓܡܐ ܐܝܬ ܒܟܬܒܐ ܗܢܐ ܕܬܪ̈ܝܢ ܕܦܘܫܩܐ ܕܐܘܢܓܠܝܘܢ ܕܠܘܩܐ܀
Item 3 (Fol. 4b): Cyril of Alexandria. The second part of the Homilies of Cyril of Alexandria on the Gospel of St. Luke, viz. hom. homilies lxxxi.-clvi [defective]
 
Title: ܟܬܒܐ ܕܬܪ̈ܝܢ ܕܦܘܫܩܐ ܕܐܘܢܓܠܝܘܢ ܕܠܘܩܐ̣. ܕܩܕܝܫܐ ܩܘܪܝܠܘܣ ܪܝܫܐ ܕܐ̈ܦܣܩܘܦܐ ܕܠܟܣܢܕܪܝܐ.
Note: Of these, however,hom. [homilies] xcvii., cxiii.-cxvi., and cliv.-clvi., are entirely lost; whilst hom. [homilies] lxxxii., xcvi., xcviii., cxii., cxxvii., and cliii., are more or less incomplete. These homilies of Cyril have been carefully edited from this manuscript and Add. 14,551 , by the Rev. Dr. Payne Smith, Oxford, 1858; and the same scholar has published an English translation in two volumes, Oxford, 1859.
