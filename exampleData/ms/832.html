Add. 17,180

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/832
Description based on Wright DCCCXXVI (2:851-853)
Date: 11th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 34 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Abraham Nephtarenus. Selections from the writings of Abraham Nephtarenus.; An extract from "the Book of Steps," Climax or Ladder.; John the monk. Selections from the writings of John the monk.; Gregory the monk. Selections from Gregory the monk.; Ephraim. An imperfect extract from Ephraim, in heptasyllabic metre.; ... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 29 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 9⅜ in. by 6¼, consisting of 34 leaves, several of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 1, 3, 7, 10, 12, 17, 19, 29, 30, and 34. The quires, signed with letters, were once at least 9 in number; but the first three and the last are entirely lost. Leaves are also missing after foll. 10, 18, 29, 31, and 33. There are from 26 to 47 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in an inelegant, current hand of about the xith cent.
