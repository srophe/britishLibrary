Add. 14,688

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/546
Description based on Wright CCXLVII (1:188-190)
Date: 12th or 13th century
East Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 80 leaves.
Features: Decoration
Wright's Subject Classification: Lectionaries
This manuscript contains: A Nestorian Lectionary from the Pauline epistles. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 96 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 ¼ in. by 6 7/8, consisting of 80 leaves, some of which are much stained and slightly torn, especially foll. 1 and 71—80. The quires, signed with letters, were originally nine in number, of which the last is wanting, and the first is imperfect, a leaf being lost at the beginning. Each page is divided into two columns, of 23 lines.

Hand 1 (major, East Syriac script): The writing is a good, regular, Nestorian Estrangela of the xiith or xiiith cent., with numerous Syriac vowels and other marks
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Decoration (Ornamentation): There are a few attempts at ornamentation; e.g. foll. 27 a, 28 a, 45 b, and 49 a.
