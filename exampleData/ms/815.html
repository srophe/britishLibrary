Description based on Wright DCCXCII (2:782-784)
Date: 9th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 113 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Jacob of Batnae. Metrical discourses of Jacob of Batnae; Evagrius. Selections from the writings of Evagrius; Isaiah, Abbat of Scete. Selections from the writings of Isaiah, abbat of Scete; Marcus the Monk. Selections from the writings of Marcus the monk; Extracts from the Lives of the Egyptian Fathers; .... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 23 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 7 1/4 in. by 5 1/8, consisting of 113 leaves. The quires, signed with letters, were 12 in number, but the first is now lost. Leaves are also wanting after foll. 75 and 103. There are from 20 to 29 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a neat, regular character of the ixth cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Foll. 1—9 and 104—113 may be of somewhat later date.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

  Incipit: ܗܕܐ ܒܪܬ ܩܠܐ ܕܐܪܒܥ ܗܝ ܙܒ̈ܢܝܢ ܕܩ̣ܪܐ ܐܠܗܐ ܚܝܠܬܢܐ. ܘܕܡܝܐ ܡܠܬܐ ܕܠܘ ܕܠܐ ܚܝܠܐ ܗܝ ܠܐܪܒܥ ܓܝܪ ܙܒ̈ܢܝܢ ܡܬܦܠܓܝܢ ܙܒ̈ܢܐ ܕܩܕܡ ܓܠܝܢܗ ܕܡܪܢ ܘܐܦ ܦܥ̈ܠܐ ܓܝܪ ܗܠܝܢ ܕܐܓܪ ܠܟܪܡܐ̣. ܥܕܡܐ ܚܘܬ ܐܪܒܥ ܙܒ̈ܢܝܢ ܐܬܓܪܘ. ܡܐ ܕܝܢ ܕܐܬ݂ܐ ܐܬܚܙܝ ܙܒܢܐ ܕܚܡܫܐ. ܏ܘܫ..

Additions

Addition 1 (Fol. 183a): Foll. 183 and 184 are paper leaves, both slightly stained and torn, containing part of the first epistle of S. John, according to the Pěshīttā version, viz. ch. iv. 13—v. 12, written in an elegant hand of the xivth cent.
