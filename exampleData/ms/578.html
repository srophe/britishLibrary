Add. 14,709, foll. 1–71

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/579
Description based on Wright CCCLXXI (1:303)
Date: AD 1336 / AG 1647
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 71 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Services for Passion Week. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 8 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 5/8 in. by 7 1/2, consisting of 71 leaves, several of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 57 and 68—71. The quires were probably eight in number. They are signed with Syriac, and by a later hand with Armenian letters. One leaf is wanting at the beginning, and there are lacunæ after foll. 68 and 70. Each page has from 17 to 20 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a large, inelegant character, dated A. Gr. 1647, A.D. 1336.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Armenian script): A later hand has signed the quires with Armenian letters.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.

This manuscript contains 8 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Add. 14,709, foll. 72–74

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/580
Description based on Wright CCCLXXVII (1:306)
Date: AD 1336
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Paper Other, 3 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Choral Books
This manuscript contains: Portions of the Services for the Sunday of the Resurrection. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Three paper leaves, about 10 1/2 in. by 7 3/8, much stained and torn (Add. 14,709, foll. 72 —74). They are exactly similar in appearance to the preceding portion of the volume (foll. l—71, no. ccclxxi).

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): Evidently written by the same hand as the preceding portion of the volume (foll. l—71, no. ccclxxi), A.D. 1336.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 72a): Portions of the Services for the Sunday of the Resurrection
 
Title: ܥܕܢܐ ܕܨ̇ܦܪܐ ܕܚܕܒܫܒܐ ܪܒܐ ܕܩܝ̣ܡܬܐ ܏ܩܕ ܨܠܘܬܐ ܕܫܘܪܝܐ

Add. 14,709, foll. 75–94

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/581
Description based on Wright CCXXXVI (1:175-176)
Date: AD 1484 / AG 1795
West Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Paper Codex, 20 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Lectionaries
This manuscript contains: Lessons from the Gospels for various occasions. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 10 items.

Physical Description

Paper, about 10 3/4 in. by 7 1/4, consisting of 20 leaves or two quires, signed with letters (Add. 114,709, foll. 75—94). There are from 21 to 24 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (major, West Syriac script): The writing is neat and regular, with numerous Greek and Syriac vowels. It is dated A. Gr. 1795, A.D. 1484.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Arabic script): See additions.
