Add. 14,598, foll. 1–3

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1574
Description based on Wright DCCLXIV (2:731-732)
Date: 10th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 3 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Moral maxims or sentences. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Vellum leaves, about 10 in. by 6 5/8, much stained and torn. These leaves are fly-leaves of Add. 14,598 . As such, fol. 3 has been numbered with the Coptic arithmetical figure ⲁ.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): These leaves are written in an inelegant, current hand of about the xth cent.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1): Moral maxims or sentences [defective]
  Subscription (Fol. 3a): ܫ̣ܠܡ ܐܡܪܘ[ܣ] ܦܠܣܘܦܐ ܝܘܢܝܐ
Note: These moral maxims or sentences are ascribed to a "Greek philosopher," named ܐܡܪܘܣ (Ὅμηρος? or Ἵμέριος? hardly Ambrose, 'Αμβρόσιος, the friend of Origen). See Sachau in the Hermes, Bd. iv., p. 74.

Add. 14,598, foll. 3–152

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1575
Description based on Wright DCCLXIV (2:731-732)
Date: 7th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Parchment Codex, 152 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Philoxenus of Mabūg. The sermons of Philoxenus of Mabūg on Christian life and character. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 10 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 in. by 6 5/8, consisting of 152 leaves, or 16 quires, the last of which is imperfect (foll. 1—152). The quires are signed with letters, and the leaves have been numbered with Coptic arithmetical figures from ⲁ (fol. 3) to ⲣⲛ (fol. 152). Each page is divided into two columns, of from 31 to 33 lines.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a neat hand of the viith century.

Add. 14,598, foll. 153–348

Date: 9th century
Unspecified Syriac script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 196 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Philoxenus of Mabūg. Works of Philoxenus of Mabūg and Gregory Thaumaturgus; Marcus the Monk. Two discourses of Marcus the Monk on the Spiritual Law and on Spiritual Life; Isaac, abbat of Scete. Discourse of Isaac, abbat of Scete , addressed to his disciple Peter; Evagrius. Writings of Evagrius; Jacob of Batnae. Metrical discourses of Jacob of Batnae; .... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 20 items.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 10 in. by 6 5/8, consisting originally of at least 24 quires (foll. 153—348), at first numbered from ܝܕ to ܝܘ, and then from ܐ to ܝܛ, but afterwards, consecutively, from ܒ to ܟܓ. Leaves are wanting after foll. 246, 283, 284, 285, 347, and 348. Each page is divided into two columns, of from 28 to 31 lines.

Hand 1 (major, Unspecified Syriac script): Each page is divided into two columns, of from 28 to 31 lines.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): See additions.
Decoration (Illustration): On fol. 321 a there is a rude drawing of a human figure, coloured black and red.
