Add. 17,178

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/830
Description based on Wright DCCCXXVIII (2:855-857)
Date: 11th or 12th century
Unspecified Syriac script ܀ Mixed Material Codex, 146 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: Collected Authors
This manuscript contains: Extracts from the Lives of the Egyptian Fathers.; Isaiah, abbat of Scete. Short extracts from the writings of Isaiah, abbat of Scete .; Evagrius. Short extracts from the writings of Evagrius.; Extracts from the Lives of the Egyptian Fathers.; Extracts from the Lives of the Egyptian Fathers.; ... In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 28 items.

Physical Description

A manuscript, about 7 3/8 in. by 4 3/4, consisting of 146 leaves, some of which are much stained and torn, especially foll. 80—82, 91—100, and 130—164. Foll. 63—80 are paper, the rest vellum. The quires, signed with letters, are 18 in number ( ܝܚ, fol. 139 a); but the volume is imperfect at the end, and there are lacunae after foll. 52, 73, 80, 110, and 130. There are from 16 to 26 lines in each page.

Hand 1 (sole, Unspecified Syriac script): This manuscript is written in a rather peculiar, angular hand, apparently of the xith or xiith cent.
