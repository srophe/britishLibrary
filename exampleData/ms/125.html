Add. 14,452, foll. 1–22

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1158
Description based on Wright LXXIV (1:49-50)
Date: 8th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 22 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: New Testament
This manuscript contains: The Gospel of S. Matthew, according to the Peshitta version. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 1 item.

Physical Description

Vellum, about 12 3/8 in. by 10, of which foll. 1 and 9 are much mutilated. Leaves are wanting at the beginning. Each page is divided into two columns, containing from 28 to 32 lines. These leaves belong to Add. 14,451* , but have been long bound up with this manuscript.

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): The writing is a fine, regular Estrangela, of the viiith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Later hands have added Syriac vowels and diacritical points abundantly in these leaves.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The larger sections have been noted on the margins in black by a modern hand.
Decoration (Rubrication): The lessons are rubricated in the text in the usual way, with a solitary exception on the margin of fol. 18 b.

Contents

This manuscript contains 1 item(s). N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 1a): The Gospel of S. Matthew, according to the Peshitta version [defective]
Notes:
  • It begins with ch. ix. 7.
  • The Ammonian sections and Eusebian canons are marked with red ink in the text of this volume, and there is a harmony at the foot of each page.

Add. 14,452, foll. 23–57, 59–152

URI: https://bl.syriac.uk/ms/1160
Description based on Wright LXXIV (1:49-50)
Date: 6th or 7th century
Estrangela script (multiple hands) ܀ Parchment Codex, 129 leaves.
Wright's Subject Classification: New Testament
This manuscript contains: The four Gospels, according to the Peshitta version. In total, including sub-sections, this manuscript contains 5 items.

Physical Description

Vellum leaves, about 12 3/8 in. by 10, of which foll. 151 and 152 are much mutilated. The quires, originally 21 in number, are signed with letters (see fol. 23a, ܗ; fol. 44a, ܛ; fol. 69a, ܝܒ; etc.). The more recent numeration is incorrect. Leaves are wanting at the end, as well as after foll. 32, 33, 53, 78, 98 and 147. Each page is divided into two columns, containing from 21 to 26 lines. Foll. 1-22 belong to Add. 14,451* , but have long been bound up with this manuscript. Fol. 58 is a modern addition and palimpsest (see Add. 14,452, fol. 58, lower text ).

Hand 1 (major, Estrangela script): The writing is a fine, regular Estrangela, of the vith or viith cent.
Hand 2 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): Later hands have added Syriac vowels and diacritical points sparingly in this part of the volume.
Hand 3 (minor, Unspecified Syriac script): The larger sections have been noted on the margins in black by a modern hand.
Hand 4 (minor, Unspecified Greek script): There is also a marginal division into sections by an old Greek hand.
Decoration (Rubrication): The lessons are rubricated in the text in the usual way.

Contents

This manuscript contains 5 item(s) including nested subsections. N.B. Items were re-numbered for this online catalog and may not reflect William Wright's previous numeration.

Item 1 (Fol. 23a): The four Gospels, according to the Peshitta version [defective]
Note: The Ammonian sections and Eusebian canons are marked with red ink in the text this volume, and there is a harmony at the foot of each page. The large red Syriac letters on the margins (e.g. fol. 29 a, ܟܘ; fol. 30b, ܠ; fol. 31b, ܟܙ; fol. 34b, ܨܚ, fol. 37 a, ܣܘ; fol. 48 a, ܚ; fol. 48b, ܝ; fol. 59 a, ܦܒ; etc.) are probably references to an index of lessons, ܦܘܪܫ ܩܪ̈ܝܢܐ once prefixed to the book, but now lost.
Item 2 (Fol. 23a): S. Matthew [defective]
Note: The only other portion missing is ch. xxvii. 31—xxviii. 5.
Item 3 (Fol. 34a): S. Mark [defective]
Note: It begins with ch. vi. 2. There is also wanting ch. xiv. 17—36.
Item 4 (Fol. 29a): S. Luke
Note: The missing portions are: ch. viii. 35—xi.51, and xxi. 26—xxii. 33.
Item 5 (Fol. 107a): S. John
Note: The missing portions are: ch. xviii. 39—xix. 13, and xxi. 17 to the end.
